## Visao Geral

Comunicacao corporativa NAO e marketing. NAO e propaganda. NAO e media buying. E disciplina estrategica que constroi-protege-projeta **reputacao institucional** atraves de stakeholders multiplos (clientes + funcionarios + investidores + reguladores + imprensa + comunidade + governo + parceiros). E o sistema nervoso comunicacional da organizacao.

Comunicacao corporativa moderna 2026 tem 4 pilares: **PESO model** (Paid+Earned+Shared+Owned), **narrativa unificada** (backbone all comms), **leadership visibility** (audiencias confiam pessoas, nao logos), **mensuracao tied to business outcomes** (impressions morreu).

Numeros 2026 mercado: R$ 30 bi/ano comunicacao corporativa Brasil (Aberje), 38% empresas aumentando orcamento, prioridades (assessoria imprensa 56% + conteudo multiplataforma 51% + eventos 40% + branded content 32% + comunicacao interna 31%), Investor Relations market US$ 3.64 bi 2026 → US$ 5.32 bi 2030, 73% B2B buyers confiam thought leadership > ads, **80% B2B discovery via AI engines** (ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity), 86% large companies disclose sustainability, LinkedIn postings com "storyteller" doubled YoY.

Esta skill e para CEO Brasil 2026 que precisa entender que comunicacao corporativa virou estrategica nao decorativa. E para CCO/CMO que precisa decidir investimento PESO. E para Head Comm/RP/IR que precisa orquestrar narrativa cross-stakeholder. E para agencia que quer entregar comunicacao 360 (ABRACOM model). ## Fundacao 1 — PESO Model + Narrativa Unificada

**PESO Model** (Paid+Earned+Shared+Owned) — Gini Dietrich 2014 Spin Sucks, agora canonical 2026 (Walker Sands, Caliber):

- **Paid** — anuncios pagos (display, search, social, native, sponsored content). Controlavel + escalavel + caro.
- **Earned** — coverage editorial conquistada (imprensa, podcast, awards, citations). Credibilidade alta + nao controlavel.
- **Shared** — social media organico, UGC, communities, advocacy. Engagement + viral + risco controle.
- **Owned** — assets proprios (site, blog, newsletter, app, evento, podcast proprio). Longevidade + controle total + lento construir.

**Integracao 2026 nao e opcional** (Caliber Corporate): "PESO integration is no longer optional; it is now vital to communication success". Cada owned content amplificado paid + ganha earned coverage + propaga shared.

**Narrativa Unificada** = backbone all comms. **2026 strongest brand advantage** (Caliber): unified coherent narrative. NAO e mensagem. E backbone whole strategy.

**Anatomia narrativa corporativa**:
- **Purpose statement** — "por que existimos alem de lucro" (Why? Simon Sinek + Jim Stengel Grow + BlackRock Larry Fink letters)
- **Vision** — "para onde vamos" (5-10 anos forward)
- **Mission** — "como entregamos" (operacional)
- **Values** — "como nos comportamos" (cultural)
- **Tagline** — compacto memoravel (B2C frequente, B2B raro)
- **Proof points** — fatos sustentando narrativa (cases, metricas, awards)
- **Stories** — narrativas humanas exemplificando

**Dont** narrative corporativa:
- Multi-purpose vacuous ("we're committed to excellence")
- Inconsistencia cross-stakeholder (investor diz X, funcionario ouve Y, cliente le Z)
- Performative purpose nao backed by action (greenwashing detectado)
- CEO contradictions narrative pessoal vs corporativa
- Updates frequentes (narrative deveria durar 5-10 anos minimo)

**Frameworks teste consistencia narrativa**:
- **3-stakeholder cross-check** — investor pitch + employee townhall + customer ad falam mesma historia?
- **CEO test** — CEO pode contar narrative em 90s sem slides?
- **Junior employee test** — analista junior consegue explicar narrative para amigo?
- **Crisis test** — narrative resiste ataque pergunta dura imprensa?

## Fundacao 3 — Comunicacao Interna 2026

**Comunicacao Interna** = strategic discipline 2026, NAO mais "intranet boletim mensal HR". E disciplina human-centered, data-driven, two-way, multi-channel.

**Top strategic goals 2026** (Contactmonkey):
1. **Employee engagement** (top goal)
2. **Leadership communication** (40%)
3. **Change management** (30%)

**Major challenges 2026**:
- Hybrid + remote work shift (frontline pre-2020 ja era challenge)
- Generational shifts (Gen Z entering, Boomers exiting)
- AI acceleration (anxiety + capability gap)
- Constant organizational change
- Small teams: **1 em 5 IC operations e single-person team**, **10% organizacoes sem dedicated team**

**Best practices 2026** (Contactmonkey + Sociabble):
1. **Personalization + segmentation** — message relevante por audience (frontline vs corporate vs sales vs engineering)
2. **Two-way dialogue norm** — town halls com Q&A real, surveys frequentes, anonymous feedback channels
3. **Data-driven** — moving beyond annual survey → continuous, data-driven, human-centered
4. **Multi-channel** — Slack/Teams + email + intranet + mobile app + video + podcast interno + Yammer/Workplace Meta + town halls
5. **Mobile-first** — 70%+ workforce checks comm via mobile
6. **Visual + video heavy** — text-heavy emails ignorados Gen Z
7. **Frontline-specific** — frontline workers (60%+ workforce muitos setores) recebem nada via desktop tools, exigem mobile-first solutions
8. **Manager cascade** — line managers sao canal #1 trust (over CEO global comms)
9. **AI-assisted personalization** — segmentacao + send-time + content recommendations
10. **Measurement** — open rate + click + sentiment + survey engagement scores
11. **Crisis comm playbook** — ready before need it
12. **Leader visibility** — CEO + execs frequentes (vs only crisis)
13. **Recognition systematic** — peer + manager + company-wide
14. **Inclusion intentional** — multi-language + accessibility + cultures
15. **Feedback loops fast** — survey → action → comm cycle <30 dias

**Tools stack 2026** (B2B SaaS):
- **Sociabble, Staffbase, Firstup, Workvivo** — IC platforms enterprise
- **Slack, MS Teams** — daily collaboration (DM ≠ broadcast — IC platforms separados)
- **Workplace from Meta** — community internal (descontinuado 2026, migracoes em curso)
- **Yammer/Viva Engage** — social internal Microsoft 365
- **Beekeeper** — frontline mobile-first
- **Contactmonkey, PoliteMail** — internal email + analytics (Outlook addins)
- **Loom, Vimeo, Wistia** — video assets
- **Glint, Culture Amp, Lattice, Officevibe** — engagement surveys + listening
- **Brasil-specific**: Feedz, Convenia, Gupy (HR adjacents), Workplaces (alternativas Meta Workplace)

**KPIs 2026 modernos**:
- eNPS (employee NPS)
- IC content engagement (open + click + reshare)
- Survey participation rates
- Sentiment analysis (text mining + Glint)
- Town hall attendance + Q&A volume
- Comm-to-action correlation (after change comm, behavior change observed?)

## Fundacao 4 — AI-Driven PR + Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

**Reset 2026 PR**: SEO PR estavel 20+ anos. **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) substitui em 2026**. Razao: 80% B2B discovery journeys via AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude), nao Google search direto.

**Caliber Corporate 2026**: "The new PR currency isn't impressions, backlinks or earned mentions—it's citations by machines. In a world where AI systems shape 80% of B2B discovery journeys, your brand's credibility will depend on whether you're trusted enough to be quoted by the algorithm itself."

**GEO mechanics**:
- AI engines treinados earned media authoritative outlets (NYT, WSJ, Forbes, Valor, Estadao, Reuters, Bloomberg)
- Brands citadas frequencia + autoritative outlets → quoted AI summaries
- Brands sem earned media authoritative → invisible AI responses
- **Earned media virou data layer AI training**

**Implicacoes praticas PR 2026**:
- **Tier 1 outlets matter MAIS** (NYT, WSJ, Forbes, Reuters, Valor, Estadao, FT)
- **Long-form > short** (AI prefere depth contextual)
- **Quotes specific + nominais** (executive name + role + statement)
- **Statistical claims sourced** (AI cites data-backed)
- **Wikipedia presence + accuracy** (AI training source major)
- **Subject matter expert positioning** (Google E-E-A-T legacy + AI mirror)

**Tactical PR 2026**:
- **Slow PR** (Media Minefield) — quality > quantity, relacionamentos genuinos jornalistas, ideias time develop, success measured strong relationships + coverage over time
- **Hyperpersonalization** (PRLab) — real-time data + AI + predictive analytics, individual outreach
- **Snackable thought leadership** — LinkedIn carousels + TikTok explainers + vertical storytelling B2B simplifying complex
- **Earned media standards rising** — sharper storytelling + stronger data + clear POV (generic press releases dead)
- **Authenticity > polish** — real people behind-the-scenes transparent (Gen Z + Millennials anti-corporate)

**Tools PR 2026**:
- **Cision, Muck Rack, Meltwater** — media database + monitoring (legacy enterprise)
- **Prowly, Notified, Onclusive** — challenger PR tools
- **AlphaSense, Sintilio** — AI-powered media intelligence
- **Brandwatch, Sprinklr, Talkwalker** — social listening
- **Helio (formerly Help A Reporter / HARO)** — journalist outreach
- **Featured.com, Connectively, Qwoted** — expert sourcing modern
- **Brasil-specific**: Maxpress, Boomerang/MediaConta media database BR; SecPress assessoria platform; MediaMonitors clipping BR

**KPIs PR 2026**:
- AI citation rate (brand citado ChatGPT/Gemini/Perplexity?)
- Tier 1 earned coverage volume + quality
- Share of voice (vs competitors)
- Sentiment (positive/neutral/negative + intensity)
- Backlink quality (DA/DR domain rating)
- Wikipedia accuracy + presence
- Conversion correlation (earned coverage → leads/signups)

## Anti-Patterns 18

1. **Vacuous narrative** — "we're committed to excellence" generico. Sem proof points, sem POV, indistinguivel competidor.
2. **Multi-stakeholder inconsistency** — investor pitch X, employee townhall Y, customer ad Z. Trust corroi.
3. **Performative purpose** — purpose claim sem action backing. Greenwashing/woke-washing detectado virou.
4. **CEO ghost LinkedIn** — account dormant. 80% B2B discovery via AI quoting personalities, CEO invisible perde share of voice.
5. **Press release mass pitching** — generic blast. 2026 jornalistas exigem sharper storytelling + clear POV.
6. **Impressions-only KPIs** — board nao satisfeito. Connect business outcomes ou comm vira budget cut target.
7. **Internal comm broadcast-only** — sem two-way. Engagement collapses. Surveys vacuous.
8. **Frontline excluded** — comm desktop-only. 60%+ workforce frontline ignorada.
9. **Annual survey only** — vs continuous data-driven. Insights stale + response fatigue.
10. **PESO unbalanced** — paid 80% sem earned/shared/owned. Pay-to-play unsustainable.
11. **Slow earned tactical** — generic press release vs slow PR + relacionamento.
12. **GEO/AI ignored** — SEO-only PR. Brand invisible AI engines = invisible 80% B2B discovery.
13. **ESG disclosure greenwashing** — claims sem materiality + sem data backing. Reputational + regulatory risk.
14. **IR scripted robotic** — earnings reading. Analysts perceive insincerity. Stock penalty.
15. **Crisis sem playbook** — improvise pos-incident. Time-to-response too slow + inconsistent.
16. **Wikipedia ignored** — major AI training source. Inaccuracies propagate AI summaries.
17. **Owned content quarterly** — vs continuous engine. Mente shareable goes stale.
