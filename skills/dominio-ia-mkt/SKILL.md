## 3. Fundacao 3 — GEO / LLMO / AEO three-layer framework AI search

### 3.1. As tres camadas — definicoes 2026

**LeadGen Economy framework** (referenced 2026):

**LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization)**:
- **Target**: training-data citation in models like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini.
- **Goal**: ser citado pelos modelos quando users perguntam (sem search engine).
- **Tactics**: structuring + tagging content para modelos understand + trust + accurately reference.

**GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)**:
- **Target**: retrieval-augmented engines like Perplexity + Google AI Overviews.
- **Goal**: ser usado como source quando AI gera resposta.
- **Tactics**: alinhar com como AI summarize + synthesize + cite content.

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)**:
- **Target**: direct extraction into answer surfaces.
- **Goal**: ser a resposta direta featured.
- **Tactics**: structured data + concise answers + question-answer format.

### 3.2. LLMO 4 pillars (early 2026 calcified framework)

**Por LeadGen Economy + EMarketer 2026**:

**Pillar 1 — Entity architecture**:
- Definir entidades claras (people, places, things, concepts).
- Knowledge graph integration.
- Schema.org structured data.
- Wikidata + Wikipedia presence (modelos training data).

**Pillar 2 — E-E-A-T amplification**:
- Experience + Expertise + Authoritativeness + Trustworthiness (Google E-E-A-T).
- Author bios + credentials visible.
- First-hand experience evidenced.
- External authoritative citations.

**Pillar 3 — Content structure for AI consumption**:
- Clear hierarchical headings (H1/H2/H3 logical).
- Bulleted/numbered lists para extraction.
- Concise direct answers para questions.
- Tables para comparisons.
- llms.txt 2026 standard (cross-skill `dominio-ti-mkt`) — Markdown documentation entry point AI tools.

**Pillar 4 — Cross-platform optimization**:
- LinkedIn + X + Reddit + Quora content (training data heavy).

## 4. Fundacao 4 — AI ethics + regulation 2026

### 4.1. EU AI Act — vigor agosto/2026 transparency rules

**Regulation EU 2024/1689** — world's first comprehensive AI law:
- **Vigor 1 ago/2024** — overall framework.
- **Praticas proibidas vigor 2 fev/2025** — already in effect (subliminal manipulation, social scoring, etc).
- **Transparency rules vigor 2 ago/2026** — **CRITICAL milestone marketing**.
- **High-risk parcial vigor 2 ago/2026** — high-risk AI systems Annex III.
- **High-risk full vigor 2 ago/2027** — completo.

**Transparency obligations marketing 2026** (Herbert Smith Freehills Kramer):
> "AI-generated text, images, video, and audio must be **labeled clearly** when it gives the impression of being fully human-made — written text, photorealistic images, synthetic voices."

**Disclosure mandatory 2026**:
- Synthetic text passing as human (blog posts, articles, ad copy).
- Photorealistic images (NOT obviously AI like cartoons).
- Synthetic voices (TTS, voice cloning).
- Deepfake video (any synthetic person).

### 4.2. NY State AI Disclosure Law — vigor June/2026

**New York General Business Law amendment**:
- **Vigor June/2026**.
- Requires advertisers distributing content NY to **conspicuously disclose** AI-generated synthetic performers.
- "Conspicuously" = clear + prominent + readable.

**Implications**:
- Marketers using AI synthetic performers (voice clones, deepfake actors, AI characters) must disclose.
- Distribution NY-targeted = trigger.
- Penalties for non-compliance.

### 4.3. Florida AI Law

**Florida AI legislation**:
- Focus deepfake prevention.

### 4.4. Lei 15.325/2026 BR (cross-skill `dominio-juridico-mkt`)

### 4.5. AI ethics best practices marketing 2026

**5 best practices ethical AI marketing**:

**1. Disclosure transparency consistent**:
- Mesmo onde nao requerido lei, disclose AI use.

**2. Data privacy + consent**:
- LGPD compliance (cross-skill `dominio-juridico-mkt`).
