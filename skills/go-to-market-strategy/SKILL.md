Decisao GTM determina margens, valuation multiples, capital efficiency, fit com investidores, capacidade escalar.

GTM 2026 tem **3 motions primarias** (PLG Product-Led + SLG Sales-Led + CLG Community-Led), **1 motion dominante** (hybrid PLG+SLG, OpenView 2024 67% empresas hybrid hit NRR vs 58% pure-PLG), **6 elementos core** (ICP + value prop + channels + pricing + sales motion + metrics), **4 mercados primarios** (SMB <$5k ARR + Mid-market $5-50k + Enterprise $50k-500k + Strategic $500k+), e **1 framework decisao motion** (ACV-based: <$5k = PLG, $5-50k = hybrid, >$50k = SLG-led).

## Fundacao 1 — GTM Definition + 6 Elementos Core + GTM Canvas

**GTM strategy** = plano coordenado de **como** entregar produto/servico ao mercado tal que crescimento seja replicavel, mensuravel, economicamente sustentavel. Diferente de **marketing strategy** (foco demand gen), de **sales strategy** (foco fechamento), de **product strategy** (foco roadmap). GTM e o **arquitetura cross-funcional** que conecta produto + marketing + vendas + customer success + finanças.

**6 elementos core GTM** (per Apollo, Bessemer, Reforge frameworks):

1. **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)** — quem compra, quem nao, criterios fit (size, industry, geo, persona, jobs-to-be-done, urgencia, budget, tecnografia)
2. **Value proposition** — promessa diferenciada vs alternatives (status quo, competitor, build in-house, do nothing)
3. **Channels** — caminhos go-to-market (direct sales, self-serve, channel partners, marketplaces, community, content/SEO, paid, events, ABM)
4. **Pricing & packaging** — modelo cobranca (subscription, usage, hybrid, perpetual, freemium, tiers, add-ons), price point, packaging logic
5. **Sales motion** — como deal e fechado (self-serve, transactional inside sales, AE-led, ABM enterprise, channel/partner-led, hybrid)
6. **Metrics** — KPIs medindo saude motion (CAC, LTV, payback, NRR, GRR, Magic Number, Rule of 40, win rate, sales cycle, ACV)

**GTM Canvas** (1-page strategic framework, similar Business Model Canvas mas focado GTM):

```
+---------------------+---------------------+---------------------+
| ICP                 | Value Proposition   | Differentiation     |
| (segments, fit)     | (jobs solved)       | (vs alternatives)   |
+---------------------+---------------------+---------------------+
| Channels            | Sales Motion        | Pricing & Packaging |
| (acquisition paths) | (motion + cycle)    | (model + tiers)     |
+---------------------+---------------------+---------------------+
| Metrics & Goals     | GTM Team Structure  | Capital Efficiency  |
| (KPIs targets)      | (roles + ratios)    | (CAC payback, R40)  |
+---------------------+---------------------+---------------------+
```

**5 perguntas estrategicas GTM** (Reforge framework):
1. Para **quem** vendemos? (ICP refinement)
2. **Como** descobrem que precisam de nos? (demand creation/capture)
3. **Como** experimentam valor? (free trial, demo, freemium, POC)
4. **Como** decidem comprar? (buyer journey, buying committee)
5. **Como** crescem como conta? (expansion, upsell, cross-sell, advocacy)

**Anti-pattern numero 1 GTM**: empresa que diz "nosso ICP e qualquer empresa que precisa de [feature]". ICP vago = motion vago = burn alto sem traction. ICP serio define exclusoes explicitas (NAO vendemos para X, Y, Z).

**GTM motion fit ACV** (Apollo/PipelineRoad benchmark 2026):
- ACV < $5k → PLG-led (self-serve, freemium, viral)
- ACV $5k-$50k → Hybrid PLG+SLG (self-serve entry + sales-assisted close)
- ACV $50k-$500k → SLG-led com PLG entry point (POC, pilot, trial-to-paid)
- ACV $500k+ → Enterprise SLG + ABM + buying committee 6-12 meses

**Outputs minimos GTM doc** (para Series A defense):
- ICP statement (1 paragrafo + criterios fit/exclusao)
- Value prop (1 frase + 3 differentiators)
- Channel mix (top 3 channels priority + budget allocation)
- Sales motion (descricao + cycle length + ACV range)
- Pricing (modelo + tiers + price points + lift assumptions)
- Metrics & targets (Year 1-3 ARR, CAC, LTV, payback, NRR)
- Team plan (headcount sales/marketing/CS por estagio)

## Fundacao 2 — PLG (Product-Led Growth) Wes Bush Reforge Framework

**PLG (Product-Led Growth)** = motion onde **produto** e principal driver de aquisicao, ativacao, expansao, retencao. Buyer descobre valor pelo produto antes (ou em vez de) falar com sales. Coined Wes Bush (ProductLed.com) e mainstream Reforge (founded Brian Balfour, ex-VP Growth HubSpot). Cases canonicos: Slack, Zoom, Notion, Figma, Calendly, Loom, Airtable, Atlassian, Datadog, Twilio, Stripe.

**3 modelos PLG** (per Wes Bush):
1. **Freemium** — versao gratuita perpetua + paid tiers (Slack, Notion, Calendly, Loom)
2. **Free trial** — periodo limitado full-feature (HubSpot, Salesforce trial, Zoom Pro trial)
3. **Reverse trial** — comeca com tudo, downgrade ao free apos N dias (Stripe, Linear, Notion mais recente)

**5 pilares PLG** (Wes Bush book):
1. Strategy — escolher modelo (freemium vs trial vs reverse trial) baseado em produto + ICP + value frequency
2. Positioning — value prop product-led (low-friction, fast time-to-value, viral)
3. Pricing — packaging tiers que migrate user free → paid baseado em behavior (limits, advanced features, team seats)
4. Acquisition — content + SEO + word-of-mouth + viral loops (referral, sharing) + community
5. Activation — onboarding flow que entrega "aha moment" rapido (TTV time-to-value)

**Metricas PLG core 2026** (per Reforge, Mixpanel, Amplitude):
- **Activation rate** — % users que completam acao chave value (defined per product)
- **TTV (Time-to-Value)** — tempo signup ate primeira value experience (PLG 2.0 benchmark < 60s)
- **PQL (Product-Qualified Lead)** — user com behavior buying intent (vs MQL marketing-qualified)
- **PQA (Product-Qualified Account)** — conta com PQLs multiplos high-value
- **Free-to-paid conversion** — % users free convertem paid (typical 2-5% mass-market, 10-25% B2B SaaS)
- **Self-serve % revenue** — % ARR sem touch sales (PLG mature 50%+; hybrid 20-40%)
- **Net dollar retention (NDR)** — expansion baseado em usage growth (PLG strong > 120%)
- **Viral coefficient (k)** — convidados/invite (k > 1 = viral exponential)
- **Engagement metrics** — DAU/MAU, session length, feature adoption

**PLG benefits comprovados** (per Bessemer, OpenView, ProductLed):
- 30-50% lower S&M cost (sem armada AE)
- 5x faster growth top quartile (per OpenView 2024)
- Lower CAC (acquisition predominant content + product-led discovery)
- Bottoms-up enterprise penetration (developers/individuals → teams → org)
- High NRR (expansion automatica via usage growth)
- Capital efficient (Magic Number > 1.5 PLG mature)

**PLG challenges**:
- Long path Series A (precisa product polish + analytics maturity)
- Free user support cost (1% conversion = 99% custo)
- Pricing complexity (packaging tiers + usage limits + add-ons)
- Enterprise security/compliance/procurement nao self-serve
- Founder mindset shift (product team owns growth)

**PLG 2.0 era 2024-2026** (per ProductLed Predictions 2026):
- AI-native onboarding (personalizado por intent + role + behavior)
- Time-to-value < 60 segundos (vs < 10 min PLG 1.0)
- Embedded AI agents in product (Linear AI, Notion AI, Cursor)
- Reverse trial mainstream (better activation que freemium puro)
- PLG + community + AI = trifecta dominant 2026
- Wes Bush 2026: "PLG is not dead, won't be — but evolving fast"

**Quando PLG fit** (decision criteria):
- Produto self-explanatory (pode demo sozinho sem sales)
- TTV curto (minutos, nao semanas)
- ACV baixo-medio (<$50k anual)
- ICP individual user ou small team (developer, designer, PM, marketer, ops)
- High volume mercado (TAM grande)
- Network effects ou viral mechanics possible
- Founder com forca product/engineering (vs sales DNA)

**Quando PLG NAO fit**:
- Enterprise complex (security/compliance/procurement)
- High ACV $500k+ (ROI negativo PLG cost)
- Custom integration heavy
- Buying committee complex (6-12+ stakeholders)
- Industry regulada (financial, healthcare, gov com long sales cycles)
- Produto nicho + ICP small (low TAM nao justifica PLG infra)

## Fundacao 3 — SLG (Sales-Led Growth) Enterprise Default

**SLG (Sales-Led Growth)** = motion onde **time vendas** lidera todo deal cycle (prospecting → discovery → demo → negotiation → close → onboarding → expansion). Default historico SaaS B2B 1999-2015 (pre-PLG era). Cases canonicos: Salesforce (1999+), Workday, ServiceNow, Oracle, SAP, Microsoft Dynamics, Snowflake (early), Palantir, MongoDB Enterprise.

**Quando SLG fit** (DealHub, Userpilot framework 2026):
- ACV > $25k-$50k anual (justifica AE quota carrier)
- Produto complex (custom integration, training, change management)
- Buyer risk-averse (financial, healthcare, gov, regulated)
- Buying committee 5-10+ stakeholders (CFO, CTO, CISO, procurement, end users, executive sponsor)
- Sales cycle 3-12 meses (vs PLG dias-semanas)
- CAC $5k-$50k justificavel pela LTV
- Top-down decision (CIO/CTO mandates, nao bottom-up adoption)

**Estrutura time SLG enterprise** (per Bessemer, Reforge):
- **SDR/BDR** (Sales Development Rep / Business Development Rep) — outbound prospecting, qualification
- **AE (Account Executive)** — discovery, demo, negotiation, close
- **SE (Sales Engineer / Solution Architect)** — technical deep-dive, POC, integration design
- **CSM (Customer Success Manager)** — post-sale onboarding, adoption, expansion, renewal
- **Account Manager (AM)** — strategic accounts expansion, named accounts
- **VP Sales / CRO** — quota management, pipeline review, hiring, comp
- **RevOps** — territory, comp design, forecast, tooling, analytics

**SLG sales process 6 stages** (canonical Solution Selling / SPIN Selling adaptado SaaS):
1. **Prospecting** — identify accounts ICP fit (ABM tier 1-2, intent signals, lookalikes)
2. **Discovery** — qualify pain, budget, authority, need, timing (BANT/MEDDIC/MEDDPICC framework)
3. **Demo & POC** — show value alinhado pain (custom demo > generic; POC para complex)
4. **Proposal & Negotiation** — pricing, T&Cs, security review, legal, procurement
5. **Close** — signed contract, kickoff
6. **Land & Expand** — initial deployment, expansion vagoes (multi-product, multi-team, multi-region)

**Frameworks qualification SLG 2026** (per Salesforce, Gong, Outreach):
- **BANT** — Budget, Authority, Need, Timing (basic 1960s)
- **MEDDIC** — Metrics, Economic buyer, Decision criteria, Decision process, Identify pain, Champion (Parametric Tech 1996)
- **MEDDPICC** — MEDDIC + Paper process + Competition (Force Management adaptation, 2026 default enterprise)
- **SPIN** — Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-payoff (Neil Rackham 1988)
- **Challenger Sale** — Teach-Tailor-Take Control (CEB/Gartner 2011)
- **Command of Message** — Force Management framework value-based selling

**SLG metrics core**:
- **CAC** — total S&M cost / new logos (SLG enterprise $20k-$200k+ por logo)
- **CAC payback** — meses recuperar CAC (top quartile < 18 meses; problematic > 24)
- **Sales cycle length** — dias 1st touch ate close (SLG enterprise 90-365 dias)
- **Win rate** — deals fechados / deals em pipeline (top 30-40%, average 20-25%)
- **ACV (Annual Contract Value)** — receita anual por logo
- **Quota attainment** — % AEs hit quota (top quartile 70%+, average 50-60%)
- **Pipeline coverage** — pipeline / quota (3-5x healthy)
- **Deal velocity** — value/dia em pipeline movimento
- **NRR** — net revenue retention (SLG enterprise expansion 110-130% top)

**SLG capital efficiency 2026 challenge**:
- CAC inflation (S&M cost rising)
- Sales cycle elongation (buying committee complexity, AI evaluation overhead)
- AE quota attainment dropping (Gartner 2025 data: 47% AEs hit quota vs 56% 2020)
- Bessemer benchmark: SLG efficient = Magic Number > 0.7, payback < 24 meses, NRR > 110%

**ABM (Account-Based Marketing) overlay SLG**:
- Tier 1 (top 10-50 accounts): 1:1 personalized full-court press, custom content, exec engagement, events
- Tier 2 (next 100-500): 1:few segment plays, intent-based outreach, custom pages
- Tier 3 (500-5000): 1:many programmatic ABM, lookalike modeling
- ABM tools: 6sense, Demandbase, RollWorks, Terminus, Madison Logic, ZoomInfo, Apollo
- Cross-skill: ver `abm` skill (sister bloco MKT 2o lote)

## Fundacao 4 — CLG (Community-Led Growth) Emergent 2024+

**CLG (Community-Led Growth)** = motion onde **comunidade engajada de usuarios/fans** drive awareness, advocacy, expansao, retencao. Emergent 2020-2024 mainstream 2026. NAO e "ter um Slack/Discord" passive. E disciplina formal com community ops + member journeys + content + events + advocacy programs. Cases canonicos: HubSpot Inbound (community + certifications), Notion creators + ambassadors, GitHub developers, Reforge alumni network, Salesforce Trailblazers, Atlassian Community, Figma Friends, Webflow community.

**Numeros 2026** (per Common Room, Orbit, CMX, GREYRADIUS):
- 58% top SaaS companies investem em community (vs 31% 2020)
- Community-sourced deals fecham 72% mais rapido vs cold inbound
- Community ROI: 3-7x investimento programmatic (Common Room benchmark)
- Average community member LTV 2-4x higher vs non-member customer
- 2026: CLG complement (nao replace) PLG/SLG, mas pode ser primary motion para developer tools, design tools, education platforms

**4 modelos CLG** (per Orbit, CMX 2026):

1. **Product community** — usuarios produto trocam tips, troubleshoot, feedback (Notion, Figma, Webflow)
2. **Practice community** — profissao/role compartilha learnings (Reforge alumni, ProductHunt makers, RevGenius RevOps)
3. **Play community** — fan/hobby ao redor brand (Apple fans, Tesla owners, Glossier Pink Friends)
4. **Purpose community** — causa/missao compartilhada (Allbirds sustainability, Patagonia activism)

**Orbit framework community love + reach** (per Orbit Model):
- **Love** — engagement depth (4 levels: observer, fan, contributor, ambassador)
- **Reach** — influence amplitude (followers, members influenced)
- **Gravity** — pulling power community (events, programs, signature content)
- **Member journey** — observer → fan → contributor → ambassador (engineered intentionally)

**CLG operacional roles**:
- **Head of Community** / VP Community — strategy, exec sponsor, comms cross-funcional
- **Community Manager** — daily ops, moderation, programming, member support
- **Community Programs Manager** — events, certifications, ambassador program, MVP program
- **Developer Advocate / Customer Advocate** — content + speaking + technical relationship building
- **Community Operations** — analytics, tooling (Common Room, Orbit, Discourse, Discord, Slack), membership data

**CLG metrics core**:
- **Active members** (DAU/MAU community)
- **Member contribution rate** (% members posting, ajudando, criando content)
- **Time to first contribution** (similar TTV PLG, mas community)
- **Net Promoter Score community** (specific NPS members)
- **Community-sourced pipeline** ($ ARR originating community signals)
- **Community-sourced product feedback** (feature requests adopted from community)
- **Ambassador program ROI** (custo programa vs deals influenced)

**CLG tools 2026**:
- **Platforms**: Discourse, Discord, Slack, Circle, Mighty Networks, Bevy (events)
- **Analytics**: Common Room, Orbit, Insided, Tribe Analytics
- **Engagement**: Threads, Reddit, Stack Overflow (developer)
- **Events**: Bevy, Hopin, Lu.ma, Eventbrite

**Quando CLG fit**:
- Produto/categoria com aspect colaborativo (developer, designer, marketer, ops practitioner)
- Profession/role com identity strong (RevOps, PM, designer, security engineer)
- Long-term LTV mindset (community payoff 2-3 anos)
- Founder/CEO advocate visivel community (HubSpot Brian Halligan, Notion Akshay)
- Capacidade investir programs + content + events $200k-$2M+/ano

**Quando CLG NAO fit como primary**:
- Produto transactional (one-shot purchase, sem ongoing engagement)
- B2C mass-market commodity
- Buyer profile non-collaborative (executivos isolados)
- Recursos limitados pre-PMF (priorize PLG/SLG primeiro)

**CLG complementaridade 2026**:
- PLG + CLG = developer tools (GitHub, Stripe, Twilio, Vercel, Supabase)
- SLG + CLG = enterprise SaaS (HubSpot Inbound + AE-led, Salesforce Trailblazers + AE)
- PLG + SLG + CLG = trifecta dominant best-in-class (Atlassian, Notion, HubSpot)

## Fundacao 5 — Hybrid PLG-SLG Mainstream 2026 (Wes Bush + OpenView)

**Hybrid PLG+SLG** = motion combinando PLG bottoms-up com SLG top-down. Mainstream 2026 default B2B SaaS mid-market+. Per OpenView 2024 SaaS Benchmarks: 67% hybrid hit NRR target vs 58% pure-PLG. Per Wes Bush ProductLed Predictions 2026: hybrid e o "novo SLG" 2026.

**Por que hybrid mainstream 2026**:
- PLG sozinho nao escala enterprise (security/compliance/procurement bloqueio)
- SLG sozinho ineficiente capital (CAC inflation 2023-2026)
- Hybrid combina PLG acquisition efficiency + SLG revenue depth
- Bottom-up signal (PQL/PQA) feeds top-down sales prioritization
- Self-serve handles long tail $0-5k ACV; sales handles $25k+ deals
- Cases canonicos hybrid: Datadog, MongoDB, Twilio, Atlassian, HubSpot, Snowflake (recent), Notion (Enterprise tier 2024)

**Hybrid framework anatomy** (per Apollo, Userflow, OpenView):

1. **Land** com PLG (free/trial entry)
2. **Trigger sales** em intent signals (feature adoption spikes, team size threshold, multiple users same domain, security request, integration setup)
3. **Sales-assisted close** AE intervention enterprise tier (security review, custom contract, volume pricing)
4. **Expand** com hybrid (auto-expansion via usage + sales-led expansion via AM/CSM)
5. **Feedback loops** sales insights → product roadmap (closed loop)

**Hybrid org design 2026** (per Reforge, Bessemer):
- Product team (PLG ownership) + Sales team (SLG ownership) + Growth team (acquisition shared) + CS team (retention/expansion)
- RevOps coordena PLG signals → sales prioritization
- Marketing supports both (PLG content/SEO + ABM enterprise)
- Tooling overlap: product analytics (Mixpanel/Amplitude/Heap) + CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot) + CS (Gainsight/ChurnZero) + RevOps stack

**ICP segmentation hybrid**:
- **SMB/self-serve** (free + paid tier $0-$5k): PLG full self-serve, NO sales touch
- **Mid-market** ($5k-$50k): PLG entry + sales-assisted close (low-touch AE)
- **Enterprise** ($50k+): SLG-led + ABM + PLG entry point (POC, pilot)
- **Strategic** ($500k+): named account SLG, multi-product land/expand, executive sponsorship

**Hybrid KPIs**:
- **Self-serve % revenue** (target 20-50% hybrid mature)
- **PQL → SQL conversion** (% PQLs handed sales que vira pipeline)
- **PQL win rate** (% PQLs viram closed-won)
- **Sales-assisted ARR** (deals que entraram via PLG mas fecharam SLG)
- **Hybrid NRR** (>120% top quartile)
- **CAC blended** (PLG CAC + SLG CAC weighted)

**Hybrid playbook 2026 example (Datadog reference)**:
- Free tier developer self-serve (5 hosts, 14-day metric retention, basic features)
- Paid tier transactional credit card $15-31/host/mo
- Enterprise tier SLG via AE quando $50k+ ARR threshold
- Multi-product expansion (logs, APM, security, RUM) via AM
- Community + content + DevRel CLG complement
- Result: $2.6B ARR 2024, 45% YoY growth, NRR 130%+, capital efficient

## Fundacao 6 — ICP Refinement + Segment + Channel Selection

**ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)** sharp = base toda decisao GTM. ICP vago = motion vago. ICP serio define exclusoes explicitas.

**ICP framework 9 dimensoes** (per Apollo, Reforge, persona-icp-deep skill):
1. **Firmographics** — industry, size (employees, revenue), geo, structure (private/public, parent/subsidiary)
2. **Technographics** — stack atual, integrations needed, data maturity
3. **Behavioral** — buying patterns, decision velocity, risk tolerance, growth phase
4. **Persona** — economic buyer + technical evaluator + end user + champion + blocker
5. **JTBD (Jobs-to-be-Done)** — functional + emotional + social jobs (Christensen)
6. **Pain points** — top 3-5 pains que produto resolve, dor severity
7. **Trigger events** — eventos que precipitam compra (new exec, funding round, regulatory change, M&A, scaling pain)
8. **Budget & authority** — orcamento disponivel, quem aprova, processo
9. **Exclusion criteria** — quem NAO atendemos (anti-ICP explicit)

**Cross-skill obrigatorio**: ver skill `persona-icp-deep` para deep-dive ICP framework completo.

**Validation ICP** (Apollo recommendation 2026):
- 15+ buyer interviews discovery problem-fit antes generalizar
- 10+ existing customers analysis (CSAT, churn, expansion patterns)
- Win-loss analysis (cross-skill `competitive-intelligence`)
- Statistical N >= 20 (avoid anedotic over-fit)

**Segment ladder 4 mercados primarios B2B SaaS**:

| Segment | ACV | Sales Cycle | Sales Motion | Examples |
|---------|------|-------------|--------------|----------|
| SMB | <$5k | 1-30 dias | Self-serve PLG | Calendly, Loom, Notion Free |
| Mid-market | $5k-$50k | 30-90 dias | Hybrid PLG+sales-assisted | HubSpot Pro, Notion Plus, Pipefy |
| Enterprise | $50k-$500k | 90-180 dias | SLG-led + ABM | Workday, Salesforce, Datadog Enterprise |
| Strategic | $500k+ | 180-365 dias | SLG named account + exec sponsor | ServiceNow, Palantir, Snowflake mega-deals |

**Channel selection framework** (per Apollo, Bessemer, OpenView 2026):

**Channels primarios B2B SaaS**:
- **Self-serve product** (PLG entry)
- **Outbound sales** (SDR/AE outreach) — ABM-enabled
- **Inbound marketing** (content, SEO, demand gen)
- **Paid acquisition** (Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads, programmatic)
- **Channel partners** (resellers, SI, MSPs)
- **Marketplaces** (AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace, Salesforce AppExchange)
- **Events** (conferences, sponsored events, owned events)
- **Community** (CLG)
- **Word-of-mouth / referrals** (advocacy programs)
- **Strategic alliances** (co-sell, OEM, embed)

**2026 channel reality check** (per Apollo, Cleverly, GTM Engineer Club):
- Single-channel outbound mortou — omnichannel baseline
- 80% outbound deals precisam 5+ touches
- Multichannel sequences geram 40%+ engagement vs single-channel
- 81% B2B buyers escolhem vendor antes falar sales (compram pre-vendido)
- Top performing teams orchestram LinkedIn + email + phone + paid + content com sequencing precisao
- AI/agentic GTM emergent (AI SDR, AI personalization at scale, intent-based prioritization)

**Channel selection criteria** (Reforge):

## Fundacao 7 — Pricing & Packaging + Revenue Model + Bessemer Cloud Index Benchmarks

**Pricing GTM critical** porque define unit economics, valuation multiple, capacity escalar. Cross-skill obrigatorio: ver skill `pricing-strategy` deep-dive (sister 2o lote bloco MKT).

**Modelos revenue 2026 SaaS** (per Bessemer State of Cloud, OpenView):

1. **Subscription pure** — recorrente fixed monthly/annual (Netflix, Slack initial, Salesforce)
2. **Usage-based** — cobranca consumo (Twilio, AWS, Snowflake, Datadog)
3. **Hybrid sub + usage** — base subscription + usage overage (51% public SaaS 2026 vs 27% 2021, Bessemer)
4. **Tiered subscription** — tiers feature-bundle (free → pro → business → enterprise)
5. **Per-seat** — por usuario (Slack, Notion, Loom)
6. **Per-unit value-based** — por output value (HubSpot per contact, ZoomInfo per record)
7. **Outcome-based** (emergent 2026 AI) — paga por resultado (booked meetings, qualified leads, AI tasks completed)

**Packaging logic** (Reforge, OpenView):
- **Good-Better-Best tiers** (3 tiers padrao)
- **Feature gating** — features avancadas em tiers higher
- **Usage limits** — soft/hard limits forcando upgrade
- **Add-ons** — modules opcionais a la carte
- **Volume discount** — escalonamento por seats/usage
- **Annual commit discount** — desconto pagar anual upfront (cash flow + commitment)

**Bessemer Cloud Index benchmarks 2026** (top quartile SaaS public):
- **Magic Number** > 1.0 (S&M efficiency: new ARR / S&M spend trailing quarter)
- **NRR (Net Revenue Retention)** > 120%
- **GRR (Gross Revenue Retention)** > 90%
- **CAC payback** < 12 meses (top); < 18 meses (good); > 24 meses (red flag)
- **Rule of 40** = growth% + free cash flow margin% >= 40
- **ARR/employee** > $200k (top quartile)
- **Free cash flow margin** > 20% mature
- **Logo retention** > 90% annual
- **Sales efficiency** (LTV/CAC) > 3x healthy, > 5x exceptional

**Numeros mediana SaaS 2026** (Bessemer):
- Mediana gasta $2.00 por $1 ARR novo (+14% vs 2023, CAC inflation)
- Mediana CAC payback 18-24 meses
- Mediana NRR 105-115%
- Top decile growth > 50% YoY com Rule of 40 hit

**Magic Number formula**: `(ARR atual - ARR anterior) * 4 / S&M spend trimestre anterior`
- > 1.0 = efficient, double down
- 0.5-1.0 = OK, melhorar
- < 0.5 = problematic, motion broken

**Rule of 40**: `growth% YoY + FCF margin% >= 40`
- Public market gold standard SaaS health
- Bessemer: top quartile > 50

**LTV/CAC ratio**: `(ACV * gross margin * (1/churn)) / CAC`
- < 3x: problematic
- 3-5x: healthy
- > 5x: exceptional
