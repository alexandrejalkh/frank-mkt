## Visao Geral

Comunicacao ESG NAO e relatorio anual decorativo. NAO e marketing verde. NAO e CSR storytelling. E disciplina estrategica institucional que conecta **financial materiality + impact materiality + regulatory disclosure + brand narrative + stakeholder trust**. E reputacao institucional codificada em frameworks auditaveis, expostos a litigation risk, e crescentemente conditional para acesso a capital.

Comunicacao ESG moderna 2026 tem 4 vetores: **disclosure regulatorio mandatorio** (CSRD EU + ISSB global + CVM Brasil + SEC US), **materiality assessment double** (CSRD) ou **single financial** (ISSB), **greenwashing avoidance + greenhushing balance**, **integrated reporting** unificando financial + sustainability. Era voluntaria GRI 1972-2023 acabou; era mandatoria 2024-2026+ comecou.

Numeros 2026 mercado: US$ 35 trilhoes ESG-mandated assets (1/3 global AUM), 86% S&P 500 + 89% large companies disclose sustainability, IFRS S1+S2 adotados/referenciados/alinhados em **20+ jurisdicoes** (UK SRS, China nacional draft, Suica reference, Brasil CVM 193, Japao SSBJ), CSRD EU mandatory para **50.000+ empresas** com Wave 1 ja reportando + Wave 2 em 2026, **61% reducao datapoints** Omnibus (de 1.100 para ~430), Brasil CVM Resolucao 193 + 217/218/219 obriga financial sustainability reports 2026 com asseguracao razoavel, B3 Novo Mercado + ISE B3 indice referencia, **62% Gen Z prefere brands sustentaveis** + 73% paga premium, greenwashing severidade alta +30% YoY, ECGT Directive vigor 27 set 2026 EU.

Esta skill e para CEO/CCO/CSO/IR Brasil 2026 que precisa entender que ESG comm virou regulatory + financial + reputational simultaneo, NAO mais voluntario marketing verde. E para founders B Corp + impact que precisam autenticidade vs. performative. E para agencias ESG que precisam dominar GRI/SASB/ISSB/CSRD/TCFD frameworks. E para conselhos que precisam supervisionar disclosure compliance + litigation risk. E para Frank-MKT virar conselheiro reputacao institucional cross-skill (esg-comunicacao + comunicacao-corporativa + employer-branding + comunicacao-crise + terceiro-setor + empreendedorismo-impacto = 6 skills Bloco Corporativo & Humanitario, esg-comunicacao 4/6).

ESG comm bem feito = capital access + premium pricing 5-15% + talent attraction + license to operate + risk mitigation + valuation lift 4-10%. Mal feito (greenwashing) = SEC/CVM/EU enforcement + class actions + brand collapse + capital flight. Nao feito (greenhushing) = invisible AI/GEO + capital access deteriora + Gen Z talent perda + B Corp competition wins.

## Fundacao 1 — ESG Framework Global 2026 (US$ 35T + 86% Disclose + 5 Fontes Value)

**ESG NAO e novo**. GRI fundado 1997 (raiz Coalition Environmentally Responsible Economies + UNEP), TCFD 2015 (Mark Carney BoE/FSB), SASB 2011 (Jean Rogers Harvard), CDP 2000, IIRC 2010, ISSB 2021 (COP26 Glasgow). **Mudanca 2026**: integrated, mandatory, financial-grade.

4. **Capital access** (cost of capital + ESG investor pool + green bonds) — empresas alta ESG rating tem cost of equity 0.5-1.5% inferior + cost of debt 0.3-0.7% inferior (MSCI + S&P + Moody's evidence). Green bonds + sustainability-linked loans + transition bonds expandem pool.
5. **License to operate** (stakeholder trust + community + government) — comunidades locais aprovam expansao + reguladores priorizam empresas compliance + governos preferem fornecedores ESG.

## Fundacao 2 — Frameworks Reporting (GRI + SASB → ISSB + TCFD + CSRD + Brasil)

**Sopa alfabeto frameworks ESG**: GRI + SASB + TCFD + CDP + IIRC + ISSB + CSRD/ESRS + TNFD + SBTi + UN Global Compact + UN SDG + Equator Principles + WEF IBC + Climate Action 100+. **Convergencia 2026**: ISSB virou baseline + GRI + ESRS interoperabilidade.

**GRI (Global Reporting Initiative) — 1997 fundado, 2016 standards modulares**:

- **Voluntario** (NAO regulatory)
- **Multi-stakeholder focus** (impact materiality)
- **Universais GRI 1+2+3** + **topicos GRI 200/300/400** (economic + environmental + social) + **setoriais GRI 11+12+13+14+...** (oil&gas + coal + agriculture + mining + finance + textile)
- **Mais usado globalmente** (10.000+ relatorios/ano, 70+ paises, 78% G250)
- **Brasil**: amplamente adotado pre-CVM 193, ainda relevante para impact reporting
- **2025 alignment** — IFRS Foundation collaboration anunciada para full interoperability ISSB
- **2026 status**: continua voluntario MAS interopera ISSB + ESRS

**SASB (Sustainability Accounting Standards Board) — 2011 fundado, 2018 standards finais**:

- **77 industries** standards setoriais
- **Financial materiality** (investor focus, similar ISSB)
- **Industry-specific KPIs** (mineracao tem KPIs diferentes de banco diferentes de varejo)
- **2022 absorvido pela IFRS Foundation** + **ISSB efetivamente absorveu SASB** + **TCFD**
- **2026 status**: SASB Standards continua referenciado dentro IFRS S1+S2, mas SASB-as-standalone descontinuado. SASB Standards permanecem util para industry-specific disclosure dentro ISSB framework.

**TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures) — 2015 Mark Carney/FSB**:

- **4 pilares**: Governance + Strategy + Risk Management + Metrics & Targets
- **Climate-only** (NAO outras dimensoes ESG)
- **Scenario analysis** mandatory (1.5°C, 2°C, 4°C scenarios)
- **2024 absorvido pelo ISSB IFRS S2** (TCFD virou baseline IFRS S2)
- **2026 status**: TCFD-as-standalone descontinuado, TCFD recomendacoes vivem dentro ISSB IFRS S2

**ISSB (International Sustainability Standards Board) — 2021 COP26 Glasgow IFRS Foundation**:

- **IFRS S1** — General Sustainability-related Disclosures (governance + strategy + risk + metrics genericos)
- **IFRS S2** — Climate-related Disclosures (TCFD-aligned + scenario analysis + Scope 1+2+3 + transition plans)
- **Single financial materiality** (investor focus)
- **Industry-based metrics** (referencia SASB Standards)
- **Effective janeiro 2024** mas adopcao gradual jurisdicao a jurisdicao
- **20+ jurisdicoes** adotando/referenciando: UK SRS, China nacional draft, Brasil CVM 193, Japao SSBJ, Suica reference, Australia ASRS, Canada CSDS, Hong Kong HKEX, Singapura SGX
- **2026 status**: BASELINE GLOBAL emergente

**CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive) — EU 2022 directive, 2024 vigor primeiro**:

- **ESRS** (European Sustainability Reporting Standards) standards detalhados
- **Double materiality** (financial + impact)
- **50.000+ empresas EU** mandatory (apos Omnibus 2026 cut, originalmente seria 50k+, Omnibus restringe a 1000+ employees)
- **Wave 1**: large public-interest entities reportando 2025 (FY 2024)
- **Wave 2**: large companies (>250 employees) reportando 2026 (FY 2025)
- **Wave 3**: listed SMEs reportando 2027 (FY 2026)
- **Omnibus simplification 2026**: corta 61% datapoints (1100 → 430), elimina voluntary, todos topicos materiality-subject
- **EFRAG junho 2025 update**: cortou 50% mandatory datapoints, double materiality simplificada, interoperabilidade ISSB
- **Limited assurance** primeiro, **reasonable assurance** depois
- **Tem efeito extraterritorial** (subsidiarias EU de empresas BR podem cair na rede)

**TNFD (Taskforce on Nature-related Financial Disclosures) — 2023 final framework**:

- **Nature-related** (biodiversity + ecosystems + water + land use)
- **TCFD-mirror estrutura** (governance + strategy + risk + metrics)
- **LEAP approach** (Locate + Evaluate + Assess + Prepare)
- **2026 status**: voluntario MAS sendo absorvido ISSB Nature standards draft (KPMG nature reporting analysis 2026)

**SBTi (Science-Based Targets initiative) — 2015 CDP+UN Global Compact+WRI+WWF**:

- **Targets validation** (corporate climate targets aligned 1.5°C science)
- **5.000+ empresas** committed (40+ Brasil)
- **Net-zero standard** 2021
- **2026 status**: standard de facto para credible net-zero claims

**CDP (Carbon Disclosure Project) — 2000 fundado**:

- **Climate + water + forests** disclosure questionnaires
- **23.000+ empresas** disclosing 2024
- **A-list** (top tier) reconhecimento
- **2026 status**: complement ISSB, supply chain disclosure leader

**Brasil regulatorio 2026**:

- **CVM Resolucao 193/2023** — adopcao IFRS S1+S2 ISSB voluntario 2024-2025, mandatory 2026+
- **CVM Resolucoes 217/218/219/2024** (29 outubro 2024) — implementacao detalhada CBPS 01+02 (Comite Brasileiro Pronunciamentos Sustentabilidade) = traducoes IFRS S1+S2
- **Asseguracao**: limited 2024-2025, **reasonable assurance mandatory** exercicios social iniciados em ou apos 1 janeiro 2026
- **Formulario Referencia 31 maio 2026** primeira data com sustainability disclosure financeiro mandatory
- **B3 Novo Mercado** (segmento alta governanca) — exige praticas ESG
- **ISE B3** (Indice Sustentabilidade Empresarial) — 2005 fundado, carteira 2026 referencia
- **Codigo Brasileiro Governanca Corporativa** (IBGC + Comite Interagentes)
- **ABRASCA + IBGC + ABNT NBR ISO 26000** — frameworks complementares
- **B3 quinta empresa antecipar relatorio sustentabilidade financeiro padrao internacional** (Exame 2026)

**Comparacao executiva frameworks 2026**:

## Fundacao 3 — Materiality Assessment (Double + Single + Process 2026)

**Materiality** = principio determinando **o que reportar e o que NAO reportar**. Topicos imateriais excluidos = relatorio focado + auditavel + decisao-util. Topicos materiais incluidos = obrigacao reporting + assurance.

**3 conceitos materiality 2026**:

1. **Financial materiality** (single — ISSB) — informacao razoavelmente esperada afetar **enterprise value** (cash flows + cost of capital + access to capital + risk profile). Investor lens.

2. **Impact materiality** (single — GRI tradicional) — informacao razoavelmente esperada afetar **stakeholders externos** (ambiente + sociedade + economia + direitos humanos). Multi-stakeholder lens.

3. **Double materiality** (CSRD/ESRS) — combina financial + impact. **Topico material se**: (a) afeta enterprise value OU (b) afeta stakeholders externos. **Reportar se qualquer um se aplica.**

**ESRS Double Materiality Assessment Process** (10 steps padrao):

1. **Stakeholder identification + engagement** — investors + employees + customers + suppliers + community + ONGs + reguladores + cientistas
2. **Activities + value chain mapping** — upstream + own operations + downstream
3. **Impact, risks, opportunities (IROs) longlist** — todos topicos sustainability potenciais
4. **Impact materiality assessment** — severity + scope + irreversibility (ambiental/social impact)
5. **Financial materiality assessment** — magnitude + likelihood (financial enterprise value)
6. **IRO scoring + thresholds** — quantitativo + qualitativo
7. **Material topics shortlist** — overlap financial + impact (Venn diagram)
8. **Stakeholder validation** — confirmar prioridades com engagement
9. **Board/management approval** — governance signoff
10. **Disclosure structure** — ESRS topical standards mapping

**ISSB Single Materiality Process** (mais simples):

1. Identify sustainability-related risks and opportunities
2. Assess financial impact magnitude + likelihood
3. Apply industry-specific lens (SASB Standards reference)
4. Disclose material risks/opportunities + governance + strategy + metrics

**Ferramentas 2026**:

- **Canonical ESG Double Materiality Tool** — framework-agnostic AI-driven
- **Deloitte AI-driven double materiality** — assessment automacao
- **Materiality Master** — comparative platform
- **Datamaran** — issue intelligence + materiality continuous
- **Sustainalytics + MSCI ESG + S&P Global ESG + Moody's ESG** — third-party materiality benchmarks
- **Workiva, Watershed, Persefoni, Greenly, Sweep, Plan A** — ESG software com materiality modules
- **EFRAG materiality assessment guidance EFRAG IG 1** — implementation guide oficial

**Erros materiality comuns**:

- **Boilerplate** — copiar materiality matrix concorrente (audit fail)
- **Stakeholder thin** — engagement superficial (5 entrevistas) vs. robust (50+ multi-canal)
- **Annual stale** — materiality refresh anual minimum (ESRS), eventos materiais trigger reassessment
- **Quantification absent** — qualitative-only sem severity/likelihood scoring (ESRS exige)
- **Value chain ignorado** — materiality apenas own operations excluindo upstream + downstream (CSRD MAJOR ERROR)
- **Reverse engineering** — comecar dos disclosures que ja faz e justificar materiality (audit red flag)

**Reconciliacao CSRD double + ISSB single workflow** (Credibl best practice):

1. Conduct **double materiality assessment** (CSRD requirement) — broader scope
2. Extract **subset financial-material** topics (ISSB requirement) — narrower scope
3. Map disclosures to ESRS + IFRS S1+S2 simultaneously (interoperabilidade)
4. Assurance providers single-pass

**Brasil context materiality**:

- CVM 193 segue IFRS S1+S2 = financial materiality single
- Mas Brasil empresas que reportam tambem GRI mantem impact materiality
- **B3 Novo Mercado** + **ISE B3** sinalizam mais para impact materiality (multi-stakeholder)
- **Pension funds Brasil** (Previ, Funcef, Petros) crescentemente double-materiality lens

## Fundacao 4 — Greenwashing Avoidance + Greenhushing Balance

**Greenwashing** = comunicar sustentabilidade exagerando, mentindo, omitindo, ou fabricando claims sem substantiation. **Crackdown global 2024-2026** (FTC + EU + UK + Brasil).

**6 sins greenwashing TerraChoice (2007 originalmente, ainda canonical)**:

1. **Hidden trade-off** — claim verde sobre 1 atributo escondendo impactos negativos outros (paper "made from sustainable forests" mas energia carbon-intensive)
2. **No proof** — claim sem evidencia disponivel (rotulo "100% natural" sem documentacao)
3. **Vagueness** — termos genericos sem definicao (eco-friendly, green, natural, sustainable sem qualificacao)
4. **Worshipping false labels** — selos auto-criados imitando certificacoes (logo verde sem padrao independente)
5. **Irrelevance** — claim verdadeiro mas irrelevante (CFC-free quando CFCs ja banidos decadas)
6. **Lesser of two evils** — claim verde dentro categoria inerentemente nao-sustentavel (cigarro organico, SUV "eco")
7. **Fibbing** (adicionado depois) — claim simplesmente falso

**FTC Green Guides US 2024+ update (em revisao)**:

- General environmental benefit claims must be substantiated
- Carbon offsets disclosed methodology + permanence + additionality
- Recyclable claims qualified (depends on local infrastructure)
- Free-of claims qualified (free-of harmful X must clarify)
- Renewable energy claims specific (matched MWh, time-matched, location-matched)
- Compostable claims qualified (industrial vs. home)

**EU Green Claims Directive 2024 + Empowering Consumers Directive (ECGT)**:

- **ECGT vigor 27 setembro 2026** — anti-greenwashing + anti-social-washing
- Generic environmental claims (climate-neutral, eco, green) **BANIDOS** unless substantiated
- Sustainability labels apenas certificacoes terceiro-parte oficialmente reconhecidas
- Carbon offset claims requirements rigorosos
- Future environmental performance commitments (net-zero 2050) backed by detailed plan
- Penalties significativas (4% turnover EU+ + remedies + compulsory disclosure)

**SEC Climate Rule US 2024** (paused legal challenges 2024-2025):
- Scope 1+2 emissions disclosure
- Material climate risks
- Governance + strategy + risk management + metrics
- Status 2026: legal challenges Court of Appeals, possivel rescissao Trump administration

**Brasil greenwashing enforcement**:

- **CADE** (Conselho Administrativo Defesa Economica) — competition law angle
- **CONAR** (Conselho Nacional Autorregulamentacao Publicitaria) — codigo etico publicitario, casos greenwashing precedentes
- **Senacon + Procons** — direito consumidor (CDC)
- **MPF + MPE** — civil litigation
- **CVM Resolucao 193** — ESG disclosure compliance

**Casos greenwashing high-profile 2020-2026**:

- **Volkswagen Dieselgate 2015+** — emissoes fraude, US$ 33 bilhoes settlements
- **H&M "Conscious Choice" 2022** — Norwegian Consumer Authority ruling misleading
- **DWS (Deutsche Bank) 2022-2023** — SEC + BaFin US$ 19M fine ESG misrepresentation
- **Goldman Sachs 2022** — SEC US$ 4M settle ESG funds
- **Aldi + Tesco UK 2024** — ASA rulings sustainability claims
- **Brasil Boticario "biodiverso"** — CONAR processos historicos
- **Ambev "garrafa retornavel"** — CDC challenges sustainability claims

**Greenhushing — risco simetrico emergente 2024-2026**:

**Greenhushing** = silenciar sobre ESG por medo greenwashing accusations + litigation. **South Pole Net Zero Report 2022-2024**: 25% das empresas com targets net-zero NAO comunicam publicamente. Razoes: medo escrutinio + politica anti-ESG US (Trump admin + Republican states) + complexidade regulatoria.

**Riscos greenhushing**:

- Funcionarios desengajam (sem orgulho institucional)
- Investidores ESG-mandated nao incluem em portfolios (capital flight)
- Customer Gen Z 62% prefere sustentavel — invisible perde share
- AI engines (GEO) nao citam empresa — invisible discovery
- Talento Millennials/Gen Z escolhe concorrente vocal
- Setor industria nao avanca (laggards beneficiam silencio)

**Balance greenwashing vs. greenhushing 2026**:

- **Comunicar SO o substantiated** (3rd-party verification + auditavel)
- **Linguagem precisa, NAO generica** (eco-friendly → reduces emissions 35% vs. 2019 baseline, verified by ERM)
- **Transparency on limitations** (acknowledging gaps + roadmap)
- **Specific vs. aspirational** (current achievements vs. future targets clearly delineated)
- **Use de selos terceiro-parte reconhecidos** (B Corp, Cradle to Cradle, Rainforest Alliance, FSC, MSC, Fairtrade, ISO 14001, ISO 26000)
- **Avoid "X% sustainable" sem definicao**
- **Roadmap publico SBTi-validated > generic net-zero**
- **Acknowledge tradeoffs explicitly** (mineracao + agronegocio + oil&gas — nao pretender ser puro)

**8 anti-greenwashing principles 2026** (consolidados FTC + EU + Council Fire):

1. **Substantiate everything** — every claim documentado + auditavel
2. **Be specific** — quantification + baseline + timeframe + geography
3. **Third-party verify** — assurance reasonable preferida limited
4. **Acknowledge limits** — honest about gaps + tradeoffs
5. **Future commitments backed** — net-zero targets SBTi-validated + interim milestones
6. **Use precise language** — banned terms (eco, green, natural, climate-friendly sem qualificacao)
7. **Recognized labels** — third-party certifications, NAO selos auto-criados
8. **Consistency cross-channel** — annual report = website = ad = social

## Fundacao 5 — Climate Disclosure 2026 (Scope 1+2+3 + SBTi + Net-Zero 2050)

**Climate** virou **dimensao ESG mais regulada** 2024-2026 (E maioria, S+G crescendo). ISSB IFRS S2 dedicada exclusivamente. CSRD ESRS E1 dedicada. CDP foco original. SBTi targets science-based.

**GHG emissions Scope 1+2+3** (GHG Protocol — WRI/WBCSD 2001+):

- **Scope 1** — emissoes diretas (fontes proprias controladas: combustao on-site + frota propria + processos industriais + leakage)
- **Scope 2** — emissoes indiretas energia comprada (eletricidade + steam + heat + cooling + market-based + location-based)
- **Scope 3** — emissoes indiretas value chain (15 categorias):
  - **Upstream**: 1 purchased goods/services + 2 capital goods + 3 fuel/energy upstream + 4 transportation upstream + 5 waste + 6 business travel + 7 employee commuting + 8 leased assets upstream
  - **Downstream**: 9 transportation downstream + 10 processing sold products + 11 use of sold products + 12 end-of-life sold products + 13 leased assets downstream + 14 franchises + 15 investments

**Scope 3 typically 70-90% total emissions** (varies industry — oil&gas 90%+ via Cat 11 use-of-sold-products, finance 95%+ via Cat 15 investments, manufacturing 60-80% Cat 1 supply chain).

**ISSB IFRS S2 climate disclosure requirements**:

- **Governance** — board oversight + management role
- **Strategy** — climate-related risks/opportunities + business model + financial planning impact + climate resilience scenario analysis (mandatory, multiple scenarios, qualitative + quantitative)
- **Risk management** — process identifying + assessing + monitoring + integrating climate risks
- **Metrics + targets**:
  - Scope 1 + 2 emissions (mandatory)
  - Scope 3 emissions (mandatory para material categorias)
  - Industry-based metrics (SASB Standards reference)
  - Climate-related targets + baseline + timeframe + assumptions
  - Cross-industry metrics (transition + physical risks + capital deployed climate + remuneration tied)

**CSRD ESRS E1 climate disclosure**:

- Transition plan climate change mitigation
- Policies + actions + targets
- Energy consumption + mix
- GHG emissions Scope 1+2+3 metric tons CO2e
- GHG removals + carbon credits
- Internal carbon pricing (if used)
- Climate-related financial effects

**SBTi (Science-Based Targets initiative) targets**:

- **Near-term targets** (5-10 anos) reducao Scope 1+2 absoluta (+ Scope 3 categorias significantes)
- **Long-term net-zero target** (alinhado 2050 ou antes — 1.5°C pathway)
- **Validation rigorosa** SBTi committee

**Contexto**: Helena 2 anos CSO Vale (R$ 350 bi market cap, mineradora global). Brumadinho 2019 trauma + Mariana 2015 legacy. CVM 193 mandatory janeiro 2026 + asseguracao razoavel. **Frank-MKT diagnostic**:
- Vale climate disclosure leader Brasil mas Tier 2 globalmente.
- Scope 3 (categoria 11 use-of-sold-products iron ore para steel) = 95%+ total emissions.
- Decarbonization steel value chain dependente customers (ArcelorMittal, Nippon Steel, POSCO, JFE, Ternium, Gerdau).
- Tailings management pos-Brumadinho — ESG positiva trajetoria + transparency upgrade.
- Comunidades indigenas + tradicionais Amazonia + Cerrado relacao tensa.

**Recomendacao Frank-MKT 12-month transition plan publication**:
1. **Q1**: Materiality double upgrade (CSRD-style) + Scope 3 deep-dive + climate scenario analysis 1.5/2/3°C
2. **Q2**: Transition plan publication (CapEx allocation R$ 60-100bi 2030 climate, R&D, suppliers engagement steel customers)
3. **Q3**: Stakeholder engagement intensify (BlackRock + Norges + Climate Action 100+ briefings + indigenous FPIC reviews + community grievance mechanism overhaul)

7. **KPIs 12 meses**: MSCI A- → AA, sustainability-linked finance R$ 2bi nova emissao, BlackRock + Norges public endorsements transition plan, climate-related litigation reduction defensive posture, employee ESG perception survey +20 points

## Checklist Contraditorio Interno (10 perguntas)

Antes entregar ESG comm work, perguntar honestamente:

1. **Substantiation test** — cada claim documentado + third-party verifiable + linguagem precisa quantificada?
2. **Materiality robust** — double materiality (CSRD-style) + stakeholder engagement 50+ multi-canal + value chain full?
3. **Scope 3 included** — categorias materiais identificadas + measured + reported (NAO ignorado typically 70-90% emissions)?
4. **SBTi-validated targets** — near-term + long-term + interim milestones + transition plan publico?
5. **Greenwashing-greenhushing balance** — substantive comm + acknowledge limits + NAO silent?
6. **CSRD/ISSB/ESRS interoperabilidade** — frameworks mapped + assurance ready + machine-readable XBRL/iXBRL?
7. **Brasil context specific** — CVM 193 + B3 ISE + ABRASCA + IBGC + ABNT + Pacto Global + Ethos integrated?
8. **Cross-channel consistency** — annual report = website = ad = social = earnings call = LinkedIn = investor pitch?
9. **CEO + Board ESG fluency** — training + capability building + KPIs tied + comite ESG meeting cadence?
10. **Litigation + assurance ready** — D&O coverage + reasonable assurance third-party + transition plan publico defensible?
