## Fundacao 1 — EVP (Employee Value Proposition) Framework 2026

**EVP** = unique value oferecida funcionario em troca de skills+experience+commitment. Cunhado por Tandehill 2003 + popularizado por Universum/Edelman.

**EVP vs Employer Brand** (Recruitics, AIHR):
- **EVP** = INTERNO. Promessa concreta funcionarios atuais e futuros.
- **Employer Brand** = EXTERNO. Reputacao mercado talento.

**EVP framework 2026 — 3 elementos** (Universum + AIHR):
1. **Pillars** — core components informados por insights cultural DNA + audience motivations (typical 4-5 pillars)
2. **Narrative** — manifesto-style paragraph emotive "sell" do que voce oferece
3. **Strapline** — phrase concise summarizing offering, focusing memorable nao detalhado

**5 categorias EVP comuns** (AIHR):
1. **Compensation** — salary, bonuses, equity, benefits financial
2. **Benefits** — health, wellness, time off, parental leave, sabbatical
3. **Career** — growth, learning, internal mobility, mentorship
4. **Work environment** — culture, leadership, autonomy, work-life balance, hybrid
5. **Company** — purpose, values, brand, financial stability, social impact

**Aspiracional vs Real test** (Edelman + PeopleScout):
"Strong employer brands sao produzidos somente quando EVP reflete o que funcionarios atualmente experienciam — polished EVP built on internal realities employees don't recognize creates cynicism, not attraction."

**EVP discovery process** (4 etapas):
1. **Listen** — qualitative interviews funcionarios + ex-funcionarios + candidatos rejeitados (sample 30-50)
2. **Research** — exit interview data + engagement surveys + Glassdoor + GPTW + competitors EVP
3. **Synthesize** — identifique themes consistentes (cultural DNA) + audience priorities (what they care)
4. **Articulate + test** — pillars + narrative + strapline draft → test 5-10 funcionarios + 5 candidatos before launch

**Anti-pattern EVP**:
- Aspiracional sem realidade (cynicism amplifier)
- Generic ("we offer competitive salary, growth opportunities, great culture") — copia competitor
- CEO/founder vision-only sem funcionarios input
- Static (deveria refresh 2-3 anos)
- Universal (sem variantes por audience: tech vs sales vs frontline)

**Numeros impacto EVP** (Talentprise + LinkedIn):
- 50% more qualified candidates
- 28% lower turnover
- 43% less cost per hire
- 49% more qualified applications

**EVP audiences segmentation 2026**:
- **Tech talent** — autonomy + tools + remote + interesting problems + compensation
- **Sales talent** — incentives + leadership + prestige + commission predictability
- **Frontline workforce** — schedule flexibility + safety + manager respect + base pay
- **Gen Z (entrants)** — purpose + DEI + mental health + flexibility + career path
- **Millennials (mid-career)** — flexibility + leadership + impact + family balance
- **Gen X (senior)** — stability + recognition + autonomy + comp
- **Boomers (exit transition)** — legacy + flexibility + healthcare

## Fundacao 3 — Glassdoor + LinkedIn Reputation Management

**Glassdoor 2026 relevance** (Employera + Mployeradvisor):
- **Major employer brand touchpoint, signal noisier + politicized**
- **2/3 candidatos** dizem management responses to reviews influence avaliacao
- **83% job seekers research reviews/ratings antes apply** (DSMN8)
- Em 2026, candidatos piece together story de multiplas fontes (Glassdoor + Indeed + Comparably + LinkedIn + careers site + interviews) NAO single source
- Algorithm consider quantity + quality + consistency reviews

**Glassdoor management response framework**:
- **Respond TODA review significant** (especially negative) — professional + on-brand
- **Cadence**: <72h response time benchmark
- **Tone**: empatia + nao defensivo + acknowledge concern + redirect (when applicable)
- **Templates** banidos — personalize cada response
- **CHRO/Head of Talent voice** — NAO generic "HR team"
- **Negative review playbook**: thank for feedback + acknowledge specific issue + share what changed/changing + invite continued dialogue
- **Positive review response** — "thank you" + amplify especificity

**Anti-patterns Glassdoor responses**:
- Defensive/argumentative
- Threatening legal action
- Outing reviewer (privacy violation)
- Generic copy-paste
- Ignoring negative reviews silent
- Over-responding apenas negative (positive reviews ignored = signal "you only care when complaining")
- Asking employees to write fake positive reviews (Glassdoor detects + bans)

**Encouraging organic positive reviews**:
- Annual employee survey + invitation review Glassdoor
- After milestones positive (anniversary, promotion, new product launch) — invite review
- NEVER pay or coerce
- NEVER batch ask (Glassdoor algorithm flags)
- Train managers respond to good 1:1 review = "we'd love your honest review"

**LinkedIn employer brand**:

- Career page tab — vagas + EVP visible
