```
   Disclosure obrigatorio se voz e IA-gerada
   Tag #AIvoice #vozIA em descricao

CODIGO CIVIL ART. 20
   Direito a propria imagem/voz

DEEPFAKE BR
   Lei 14.811/2024 — penaliza deepfake fraudulento
   Voice cloning fraudulento = crime

CONSENT WORKFLOW
   1. Termo de cessao de voz assinado
```

## 7. Use cases marketing — operacionais

### 7.1 Jingle marca (30-60s)

```
WORKFLOW

1. BRIEF (2-3 dias)
   - Brand attributes (3 adjectives)
   - Target audience
   - Mood (uplifting, sophisticated, fun)
   - BPM target (90-110 chill, 120-130 energetic)

   - Mastering
   - Add audio logo no final 3-5s

6. DELIVERY
   - Master WAV + MP3 + Stems
   - 30s + 15s + 6s versions
   - Ringtone/audio logo standalone
```
