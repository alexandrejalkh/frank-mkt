## 2. Fundacao 2 — Weak Signals + Strategic Foresight

### 2.1. Weak signals definition

**Weak signals** (Igor Ansoff 1975 + Wendy Schultz):
- "Gross, unstructured, fragmented, incomplete, inadvertent environmental data".
- Refined into **valuable information** + **strategically actionable knowledge**.
- Emerging issues analysis identifies + watches.

### 2.2. STEEP Framework

**5 categorias trends** (foresight standard):
- **Social** (demographics, values, lifestyle).
- **Technological** (innovation, infrastructure, adoption).
- **Economic** (growth, inflation, employment).
- **Environmental** (climate, sustainability, resources).
- **Political** (regulation, geopolitics, policy).

**Used by**: governments + corporations + foresight consultancies.

### 2.3. Wendy Schultz methodology

**Wendy Schultz** (Jigsaw Foresight + Infinite Futures + OECD Expert Group):
- 6 key activities futures research.
- Dozens of tools per activity.
- Interconnected + intermixed + layered.

**Manoa method** (Schultz 2015):
- "The future is not binary".
- Multiple futures scenarios.
- STEEP categories foundation.

### 2.4. Horizon scanning

**Process systematic**:
- Scan **wide range sources** (academic + media + social + government + niche).
- Identify **emerging issues** + signals.
- Track **evolution over time**.
- **Distill weak signals → strong trends**.

**Typical sources**:
- Academic journals.
- Patent filings.
- Niche blogs + subreddits.
- Trade publications.
- Government reports.
- Conference proceedings.

## 7. Fundacao 7 — Common Mistakes + Best Practices

### 7.1. 10 erros comuns trendwatching 2026

1. **Confunde trend com fad** (duration filter).
2. **Single source** (vs triangulation).
3. **Confirmation bias** (look for evidence supporting hypothesis).
4. **Recency bias** (last 6 months overweighted).
5. **Western-centric** (ignoring BR/LatAm/Asia signals).
6. **Search-only** (vs search+social combined).
7. **Mainstream coverage** (vs niche/early adopters).
8. **AI tools blind trust** (verify human curation).
9. **No hypothesis testing** (qualitative validation).
10. **Brasil ignored** (WGSN+Mintel BR-specific).
