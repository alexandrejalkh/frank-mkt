- "Cultura nao e o que escrito website institucional — e o que VIVIDO internamente" (BR sources).

- Selection processes + comunicacao + feedback + lideranca = parte da experiencia + perception employer brand.

**Pilar 4 — Stability + financial health signaling 2026**:
- 2026 candidates weighing **stability** mais heavily.
- Concerns sobre employer financial health + potential layoffs increased since 2023.
- Continued emphasis leadership + culture.

## 3. Fundacao 3 — Gen Z workforce 2026

**Categoria 3 — Technology + communication**:
- Seamless intuitive always-on tools.
- High-quality 360 cameras hybrid meetings.
- AI/automation support productivity.

**Categoria 4 — Well-being holistic**:
- Mental-health days.
- After-hours communication boundaries.

**Categoria 5 — Purpose + values alignment**:
- Company values match daily actions.

### 3.4. Retention strategies Gen Z 2026

**Strategy 2 — Mentorship + in-office purpose**:

### 4.2. ML attrition models — como funciona

**Inputs ML attrition models**:
- Tenure + performance trajectory.
- Engagement survey responses.
- Compensation positioning vs market.
- Promotion timing.
- Manager change frequency.
- External job market conditions.
- Slack/email pattern (frequency + sentiment).
- Calendar density (overload signals).

**Output**: individual flight risk score (0-100%).
**Identifies risk weeks/months before voluntary departure**.

### 4.3. ROI documented case study

**Case 3000-person professional services firm** (TheHireHub):
- Predictive attrition model identified **340 employees at high attrition risk**.
- **78% confidence**.
- Reduced voluntary attrition **19% → 13%** in modeled cohort.
- **Estimated savings $2.1 million** vs program cost **$280.000**.
- **ROI 7.5x**.
