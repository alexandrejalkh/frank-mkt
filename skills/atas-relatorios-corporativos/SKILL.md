## 2. Fundacao 2 — Board Meeting Minutes — Estrutura legal + best practices

### 2.1. Board minutes — legal record

**Importância legal**:
- **Official corporate record** (legal evidence).
- **Director liability protection** (decision documentation).
- **Compliance audit trail**.
- **Litigation defense** (proper minutes = legal shield).

**BR specific**:
- **Lei 6.404/76 (Sociedades Anônimas)** — atas mandatory.
- **CVM Resolution 80/2022** — atas listadas requisitos.
- **Junta Comercial** registration required atas certain decisions.

### 2.2. Estrutura padrão board minutes 2026

**8 elementos obrigatórios**:

**1. Meta-information** (header):
- Company name + CNPJ.
- Meeting type (board / committee / annual).
- Date + time + location (or virtual platform).
- Quorum confirmed.
- Recording disclosure if applicable.

**2. Attendees**:
- Directors present (in-person + virtual).
- Directors absent + reason.
- Guests invited (with role).
- Secretary recording.

**3. Agenda items**:
- Numbered sequentially.
- Reference to advance materials.

**4. Discussion summary** (NOT verbatim):
- Key points raised.
- Different perspectives represented.
- NOT quotation specific directors (general practice).

**5. Resolutions/decisions**:
- Specific wording resolution.
- Vote count (in favor + against + abstain).
- Recused directors (conflict of interest).
- Effective date.

**6. Action items**:
- Specific tasks.
- Owner assigned.
- Deadline.

**7. Next meeting**:
- Date if scheduled.

**8. Sign-off**:
- Chair signature.
- Secretary signature.
- Date approved.

### 2.3. What NOT to record (CRITICAL)

**5 items typically excluded**:

**1. Verbatim director quotes** (general):
- Risk litigation (taken out of context).
- Discussion summary > verbatim.
- **Exception**: dissent specific decision documented.

**2. Privileged legal advice**:
- Attorney-client privilege protection.
- "Discussion of legal matters with counsel" sufficient.
- NOT detailed legal analysis.

**3. Personal director conflicts** (operational):
- Recusal noted (conflict of interest).
- Reasons NOT detailed.

**4. Strategic planning details premature**:
- Aspirational goals NOT yet board-approved.
- M&A targets pre-LOI.

**5. Sensitive personnel matters detailed**:
- HR investigations details.
- Specific employee performance reviews.

- "**5 items NOT to record** — risk management board minutes".
