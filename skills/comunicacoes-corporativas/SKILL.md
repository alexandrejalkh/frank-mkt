## 3. Fundacao 3 — Crisis Communication 2026

### 3.1. Crisis 2026 reality — adaptable plan

**Major shift** (UPRAISE PR, Brand24):
- Plan must be **adaptable** evolving platforms + regulatory + AI-enabled threats.
- **Reputational risk doesn't wait business hours**.
- Manipulated video + coordinated misinformation + viral customer post → escalate before leadership briefed.
- Narratives form quickly + audiences react instantly.

### 3.2. Time critical 2026

**Speed mandatory**:
- **Move within hours** (NOT days).
- Audience won't wait forever.
- **Couple of hours posting question/complaint** target.
- Faster response → shape narrative + limit impact.

### 3.3. Cross-functional command center

**Critical 2026**:
- Crisis NOT just PR function.
- **Single command center**: PR + marketing + legal + CS + technology aligned.
- **Pre-approved messaging + clear escalation tiers**.
- War room mentality.

### 3.4. Transparency + Authenticity > Silence

**Critical principle**:
- **Silence/spin doesn't work 2026**.
- Audiences expect clear + human + fast.
- Acknowledge issues early + communicate empathy.
- Brands transparent **retain customer trust** vs silent OR generic.

### 3.5. Channel strategy matters

**Where matters as much as what**:
- Wrong channel = confused message OR missed audience.
- Crisis Twitter ≠ crisis LinkedIn ≠ crisis press release.
- Channel-native messaging.
- Cross-channel consistency essential.

### 3.6. 7-step crisis management 2026 (Brand24)

**Standard playbook 2026**:

**Step 1 — Monitor + Alert**:
- 24/7 monitoring social + media + reviews.
- Alert thresholds.
- Brand24 + Meltwater + Sprout Social tools.

**Step 2 — Assess severity**:
- Levels 1-5 (minor → existential).
- Escalation criteria.

**Step 3 — Activate command center**:
- Cross-functional team assembled.
- War room virtual OR physical.

**Step 4 — Investigate facts**:
- Internal investigation rapid.
- Legal review.
- Spokesperson briefing.

**Step 5 — Communicate stakeholders**:
- Hierarchy: employees → customers → investors → media → public.
- Channel per audience.
- Messaging consistent core.

**Step 6 — Monitor sentiment**:
- Real-time tracking response reception.
- Adjust messaging if needed.
- Identify evolving narratives.

**Step 7 — Post-crisis review**:
- Lessons learned documented.
- Plan updates.
- Reputation recovery plan.
