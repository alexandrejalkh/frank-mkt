## 4. Channel architecture — 6-10 lean launch

### 4.1 Lean structure (DAY 1)

```
ANNOUNCEMENTS (read-only)
   - Releases produto
   - Eventos community
   - Updates importantes

WELCOME / INTROS
   - Newcomers post intro
   - Onboarding bot mensagem
   - Member-of-week recognition

GENERAL CHAT
   - Off-topic friendly
   - Default channel default

QUESTIONS / HELP
   - Onde noobs sentem safety (Edmondson Stage 2)
   - Older members ajudam (advocacy)
   - Founder visible periodicamente

SHOW & TELL
   - UGC posts members
   - "What I built with [Product]"
   - Recognition driver

FEEDBACK
   - Product feedback structured
   - Feature requests
   - Bug reports (low-friction)

EVENTS
   - AMA founder
   - Community calls
   - Workshops/webinars

[+ 1-3 specific channels for vertical/niche]
```

### 4.2 Anti-patterns architecture

```
NAO FAZER:

❌ 20+ channels DAY 1
   = empty channels = ghost town signal
   = newcomers overwhelmed
   = engagement diluted

❌ Promo-only channel #deals
   = audience filters out
   = trust collapse signal

❌ Sub-categories before usage justifies
   = #beginner-questions + #intermediate-questions
     antes de any volume = empty channels

❌ NSFW channels mainstream brand
   = brand risk

❌ Sem moderation channel staff-only
   = no escalation path

✅ DO:
   Start lean 6-8 channels
   Add channels APENAS quando usage justifies
   "When the question becomes a category" rule
```

### 4.3 Maturity progression

```
PHASE 1 — LAUNCH (Month 0-3)
   6-8 channels lean
   Founder very visible
   Goal: 100 active members

PHASE 2 — GROWTH (Month 3-12)
   Add 2-4 channels (especificos emergem)
   Mod team 1-2 members trusted
   Goal: 500-1000 active

PHASE 3 — SCALE (Month 12-36)
   10-15 channels (verticals, regions, advanced topics)
   Mod team 3-7 members
   Events programmatic
   Goal: 2000-10000 active

PHASE 4 — MATURE (Month 36+)
   15-25 channels (categorized)
   Sub-communities (regional, language, industry)
   Mod team 10+
   Community manager FT
   Goal: 10k-100k active
```

## 5. UGC programs — incentivos + ROI + ethics

### 5.1 Estrutura UGC program

```
TIER 1 — ORGANIC UGC (free)
   Members posting voluntariamente
   Hashtag brand (#built-with-X)
   Resharing community posts
   Recognition (Member of Week)
   COMP: zero $$$
   ROI: brand asset compounding

TIER 2 — INCENTIVED UGC ($-$$$ light)
   "Post a video using [Product] →
    enter monthly drawing for $500"
   "Top 10 UGC posts win [reward]"
   Affiliate links (% commission)
   Free tier upgrades
   COMP: $200-2k/mes total program
   ROI: -30-50% CPA vs paid

TIER 3 — STRUCTURED CREATOR PROGRAM ($$$)
   Selected creators recurring content
   Monthly retainer $600-900
   Performance bonuses (+10% engagement = +X)
   Exclusive access (beta, products)
   1-on-1 brand contact
   COMP: $5-50k/mes (10-50 creators)
   ROI: -40-60% CAC + 2-3x conversion

TIER 4 — ENTERPRISE INFLUENCER ($$$$)
   Macro-influencers
   Long-term contracts
   Co-creation (collabs, products)
   COMP: $20k-500k+/quarter
   ROI: brand awareness lift + halo effect
```

```
   - 30-day retention member

   - Members vs non-members LTV
   - = CLEAREST ROI INDICATOR CLG

REVENUE IMPACT
   - Community-sourced pipeline
   - Influence on closed deals
   - Expansion revenue from members
   - Support cost deflection

CONTENT
   - UGC volume per month
   - SEO impact community threads
   - LLM citations from community content (2026 NEW)

SENTIMENT
   - NPS community-specific
   - Sentiment analysis (auto + manual)
   - Returning participant rate
```

### 7.2 Healthy community benchmarks

```
SMALL (100-500 members)
   Active rate: 20-30%
   Daily messages: 20-100
   Founder visible: weekly
   Mod team: 0-2

MEDIUM (500-5000)
   Active rate: 25-40%
   Daily messages: 100-1000
   Mod team: 2-5
   Events: monthly minimum

LARGE (5000-50000)
   Active rate: 15-25% (dilution natural)
   Daily messages: 1000-10000

MASSIVE (50000+)
   Active rate: 5-15%
   Mod team: 20+

   Multiple sub-communities + verticals
```

## 8. Moderation = trust infrastructure

### 8.1 Filosofia 2026

```
"MODERATION NAO E COST CENTER, E TRUST INFRASTRUCTURE"

1 BAD-FIT MEMBER = damage culture inteira
   - Toxic comment killing thread
   - Promo spam degrading signal
   - Disrespect afastando newbies (Stage 1 Edmondson)

INVESTIMENTO MOD TEAM
   - 100-500 members: 0-2 mods
   - 500-5000: 2-5 mods part-time
   - 5000-50000: 5-20 mods + lead
   - 50000+: 20+ mods + community manager FT

COMP MOD VOLUNTEERS
   - Free product / premium tier
   - Recognition (mod role + badge)
   - Direct line founder
   - Conference invites
   - Some brands pay $200-1000/mes part-time
```

### 8.2 Moderation policy template

```
RULES (visible #rules pinned)

1. RESPECT ALL MEMBERS
   No personal attacks
   No discriminatory language
   No harassment

2. NO SELF-PROMO sem permission
   Off-topic links fora promo channel
   Self-promo channel se existir, com regras

3. NO SPAM
   Excessive emojis/messages
   Cross-channel posting same message
   Bot promo

4. STAY ON TOPIC
   Per channel topic
   #off-topic existe para tangents

5. NO CONFIDENTIAL INFO
   Customer data
   Internal company info
   PII

ENFORCEMENT TIERS
   1. Friendly DM warning
   2. Public message correction
   3. Timeout 24h
   4. Timeout 7d
   5. Permanent ban

APPEAL PROCESS
   DM to lead mod within 7 days
   Review by 2+ mods
   Decision communicated transparent
```

### 8.3 Crisis management

```
QUANDO SHIT HAPPENS

1. FOUNDER POSTS PUBLIC POSTMORTEM
   What happened
   What we did
   What we learned
   What changes
   = CHALLENGER SAFETY (cf. Edmondson Stage 4)

2. AFFECTED MEMBERS DM PERSONAL
   Acknowledge
   Compensate if applicable
   Personal apology

3. POLICY UPDATES TRANSPARENT
   Show changes
   Welcome feedback

4. CELEBRATE INTELLIGENT FAILURE
   "Right Kind of Wrong" Edmondson 2023
   Public learning = trust builder
```

### 10.1 Stage 1 — Inclusion safety (Months 0-6)

```

WELCOME FLOW
   - Bot mensagem custom (NAO cold "read rules")

   - Tour video (60-90s) channels overview

INTROS CHANNEL
   - Each newcomer posts intro

INCLUSIVE LANGUAGE
   - Avoid jargon assumptive
   - Multiple languages se applicable
   - Pronouns optional roles

NEWCOMER VISIBILITY
   - "New members this week" channel
   - Member-of-week recognition
   - Founder visible weekly
```

### 10.2 Stage 2 — Learner safety (Months 6-18)

```
PRACTICAS COMMUNITY

#NEWBIES-QUESTIONS DEDICATED
   - Separate from main (psychological safety)
   - Pinned "no question is dumb here"
   - Friendly mods/members tagged

TUTORIAL LIBRARY
   - #tutorial-tuesday weekly content
   - Beginner content visible top
   - Search-optimizada (forum search/Discord)

MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
   - 1 senior member = 1 newcomer 30 dias
   - Optional opt-in
   - Recognition for mentors

OFFICE HOURS
   - Founder/staff weekly office hours
   - Beginners encouraged ask
```

### 10.3 Stage 3 — Contributor safety (Months 18-36)

```
PRACTICAS COMMUNITY

UGC PROGRAMS (cf. Sec 5 desta skill)
   - Show & tell channel
   - Tutorial submission program
   - Member spotlights

CONTRIBUTION PATHS DOCUMENTED
   - Write tutorial → recognized
   - Answer N questions → mod consideration
   - Beta test → exclusive access
   - Host AMA → branded swag

RECOGNITION RITUALS
   - Founder weekly "shoutout Friday"
   - Member-of-month
   - Public LinkedIn shoutout
   - Branded swag for top contributors

LOW-STAKES OPPORTUNITIES
   - "Share your setup" channel
   - "Feedback on [thing]" polls
   - Community votes on features
```

### 10.4 Stage 4 — Challenger safety (Months 36+)

```
PRACTICAS COMMUNITY

PUBLIC ROADMAP COMMENTING
   - GitHub Issues / Canny / Productboard linked

   - Status visible (in-review, accepted, rejected)

#RFC-DISCUSSIONS CHANNEL
   - Technical/product debates open
   - Founder participates
   - "Disagree better" framework

FOUNDER AMA MONTHLY
   - Critique welcome explicit
   - Hard questions surfaced
   - Transparent answers OR "we don't know"

CRISIS MANAGEMENT TRANSPARENTE
   - Public postmortems
   - Intelligent failures celebrated
   - Apology + fix when needed

BETA FEEDBACK CHANNELS
   - Real product critique encouraged
   - Negative feedback acted upon visibly
```

## 14. Regras de Ouro

1. **NAO CHUTAR** — community sem PMF = wasted effort.
2. **Clear job** da community antes de plataforma.
3. **Lean architecture** 6-8 channels DAY 1 (cresce com usage).
4. **Trust first + structure second + promotion third** (Discord 2026).
5. **Activation rate 20-30%** saudavel; <10% = ghost town.
6. **Retention members vs non-members** = clearest CLG ROI indicator.
7. **Moderation = trust infrastructure** (NAO cost center).
8. **UGC ROI**: +161% conversions, -50% CPC, +4x clicks.
9. **Ambassador program**: -40-60% CAC vs paid + 2-3x conversion.
10. **Comp tiers**: $600-900/mes retainer micro 2026 standard.
11. **Edmondson 4 stages** progressao timeline (cf. `persuasao-eticas` Sec 5).
12. **Cialdini Unity** = community foundation (cf. `persuasao-eticas` Sec 3.7).
13. **Founder visible** weekly minimum (absente 4+ sem = neglect).
14. **Disclosure obrigatorio**: Lei 15.325/2026 BR + FTC US + GDPR EU.
15. **Micro-communities thriving** 2026 (smaller > mass para engagement deeper).
16. **1 bad-fit member** can damage culture inteira — moderation rigorosa.
17. **Recognition rituals** weekly (member-of-week, shoutouts).
18. **Cases study** Atlassian/Salesforce/HubSpot/Notion/Figma/Duolingo/Zapier/Shopify.
