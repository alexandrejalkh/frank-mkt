## Fundacao 1 — Empreendedorismo de Impacto Framework (Definicao + Spectrum + Theory of Change)

**Definicao canonica 2026** (B Lab + GIIN + Aspen Institute consensus):

> Empresa que pursue **intencionalmente** + **mensuravelmente** + **com adicionalidade** impacto social ou ambiental positivo COMO core do modelo de negocio (nao side effect), gerando retorno financeiro sustentavel.

**3 testes canonicos** (Impact Management Project IMP):
1. **Intentionality** — impacto e propositadamente buscado vs incidental? (intentional vs incidental)
2. **Measurability** — impacto e mensuravel via metricas verificaveis? (theory of change explicita)
3. **Additionality** — sem esse negocio o impacto NAO ocorreria? (counterfactual test)

**Spectrum of capital** (Bridges Fund Management):

| Tipo | Retorno financeiro | Intencao impacto | Exemplo |
|------|-------------------|------------------|---------|
| Traditional | Maximo | Nenhuma | Hedge fund S&P |
| Responsible | Mercado | Evita harm (negative screen) | ESG fundo passivo |
| Sustainable | Mercado | Considera ESG material | Fundo ESG ativo |
| **Impact-driven** | **Mercado** | **Intentional + measurable** | **Vox Capital, MOV, Positive Ventures** |
| **Impact-first** | **Sub-mercado aceito** | **Impacto > retorno** | **Yunus Social Business, Sitawi blended** |
| Filantropia | Zero/negativo | Impacto puro | Fundacao Lemann, Itau Social |

**Theory of Change** (ToC) framework canonico (Aspen, Acumen, IRIS+):

```
Inputs (recursos) → Activities (acoes) → Outputs (entregas) → Outcomes (mudancas) → Impact (transformacao sistemica)
```

Founder de impacto deve articular ToC em 1 pagina, com:
- **Problema** definido sistemicamente (nao sintoma)
- **Beneficiarios primarios** identificados (NAO "humanidade")
- **Atividades especificas** alinhadas
- **Outcomes mensuraveis** com metricas IRIS+
- **Impact assumptions** explicitas (o que precisa ser verdade)
- **Counterfactual** (o que aconteceria sem voce)

**ODS / Sustainable Development Goals 2030** (UN 17 goals + 169 targets) — language franca impact investing 2026. Founder deve mapear negocio para 1-3 ODS principais + 2-3 secundarios. NAO mapear todos 17 (impact-washing flag).

**Modelos de negocio impact-driven** (taxonomia G8 + Quintessa):
1. **B2B selling outcomes** (pay-for-success) — receita SO se outcome verificado (ex: outcome bonds educacao)
2. **Marketplace inclusivo** — conectando supply chain marginalizada (ex: Justa, ContaAzul base piramide)
3. **Tech for good** — software para terceiro setor / governo (ex: PicPay Solidario, Vakinha)
4. **Negocio inclusivo** — produto/servico essencial para baixa renda com unit economics positivos (ex: 99 Walmart Brasil microcredito, Banco Pan inclusivo)
5. **Cleantech / climate** — solucao tecnologica problema ambiental (ex: Solfacil energia solar, Ambipar)
6. **Healthtech inclusivo** — saude acessivel populacao SUS-dependente (ex: Dr. Consulta, Memed)
7. **Edtech inclusivo** — educacao acesso baixa renda (ex: Descomplica, Stoodi)
8. **Agtech regenerativa** — agricultura familiar + regenerativa (ex: Cromai, Solinftec)

## Fundacao 2 — B Corp Certification (B Lab 2006 → V2.1 Standards 2026)

**B Lab** founded 2006 Pennsylvania EUA por Jay Coen Gilbert + Bart Houlahan + Andrew Kassoy. Mission: redefinir sucesso empresarial. **9.000+ certified globalmente** 2026, 90+ paises, 150+ industrias. **Brasil 200+ B Corps** (Natura primeira gigante listada B Corp do mundo desde 2014, Boticario, Mover Participacoes, Movile, Cuesta, Insider, Korin, Naturaleza, Maria Brasileira).

**Diferenca B Corp vs Benefit Corporation** (confusao comum):
- **B Corp** = certificacao privada B Lab (auditoria externa, recertify a cada 3 anos, taxa anual escalada por receita)
- **Benefit Corporation** = forma legal corporativa (legislada estado-a-estado EUA, no Brasil emerge via Sociedade de Beneficio art. proposto). Empresa pode ser B Corp sem ser Benefit Corp e vice-versa.

**B Impact Assessment (BIA)** — ferramenta gratuita auto-aplicada:
- 200+ perguntas
- 5 areas (V1 legacy): Governance + Workers + Community + Environment + Customers
- Score 0-200 pontos, **80 pontos minimo certificacao** (V1)

**V2.1 Standards (abril 2025 → certificacao 2026)** — maior reset historia B Lab:

Mudanca fundamental: **points-based → mandatory requirements**. Antes empresa podia compensar fraqueza em uma area com forca em outra (80 pontos cumulativos). Agora TODAS empresas devem atender requisitos verificaveis em 7 areas.

**Estrutura V2.1 dois niveis**:
1. **Foundation Requirements** (universal, prerequisitos): legal incorporation + operational history + compliance + transparency + stakeholder governance (legal commitment fiduciario stakeholders nao apenas shareholders)
2. **Impact Topic Requirements** (7 areas):
   - Purpose & Stakeholder Governance
   - Fair Work (compensation + benefits + safety)
   - Justice, Equity, Diversity & Inclusion (JEDI)
   - Human Rights (supply chain due diligence)
   - Climate Action (net zero targets + Scope 1/2/3 emissions)
   - Environmental Stewardship & Circularity
   - Government Affairs & Collective Action

**Recertify cycle 3 anos** com BIA atualizada. Empresas existentes em 2025/2026 transicionam V1 → V2.1 ate 2027-2028.

**Best for the World** ranking — top 5% B Corps por area de impacto, anual.

**Pricing 2026**:
- Submission fee: ~US$ 250-500
- Annual certification fee: escalada por receita (US$ 500 receita <US$ 1M ate US$ 50.000+ receita >US$ 1B)

**Cases Brasil B Corp icone**:
- **Natura** — primeira gigante listada B Corp do mundo (2014), holding Natura&Co incorporou Avon + Aesop + The Body Shop, Compromisso Natura Equilibrio Ambiental + relatorio integrado lider America Latina
- **Boticario Compromisso** — programa social+ambiental amplo, B Corp em fase analise familia Grupo Boticario
- **Mover Participacoes** (controladora AmBev) — B Corp 2024, holding industrial gigante
- **Movile** (PlayKids + iFood histories) — B Corp tech
- **Insider** — B Corp athleisure DNVB, modelo direto consumidor Brasil

**Quando NAO buscar B Corp**:
- Empresa <2 anos operacao (foundation requirement)
- Pre-PMF (foco produto vs certificacao)
- Setor controvertido (tabaco, armas, gambling — auto-disqualified)
- Sem capacidade orcamento taxa anual + tempo BIA (200+ horas pessoa primeira vez)

## Fundacao 3 — Impact Investing 2026 (GIIN US$ 1,5T AUM + IRIS+ 600+ Metricas)

**Global Impact Investing Network (GIIN)** founded 2009 Rockefeller Foundation. Define impact investing:
> "Investments made with intention to generate positive measurable social and environmental impact alongside financial return"

**Numeros 2026 (GIIN Annual Survey)**:
- US$ 1,5 trilhoes AUM impact investing globally (estimado 2026)
- 3.000+ impact investors mapeados
- Crescimento 18% CAGR ultimos 5 anos
- Asset classes: private equity (35%) + debt (28%) + public equities (15%) + real assets (12%) + outros (10%)

**IRIS+ system** (Impact Reporting and Investment Standards) — public good GIIN, free:
- 600+ standardized metrics
- 17 themes alinhados ODS
- 5 Dimensions of Impact (Impact Management Project IMP):
  1. **What** — quais outcomes estao ocorrendo + importancia para stakeholders
  2. **Who** — quem experiencia outcomes (geografia, demografia, underserved)
  3. **How Much** — escala + profundidade + duracao
  4. **Contribution** — additionality (sem essa intervencao teria ocorrido?)
  5. **Risk** — probabilidade impact NAO ocorrer

**Core Metrics Sets** — shortlists IRIS+ pre-selecionadas por theme (educacao, saude, clima, inclusao financeira, agricultura sustentavel, etc).

**Spectrum returns first → impact first**:
- **Returns first** — retorno mercado prioridade, impact constraint (ex: ESG funds public)
- **Returns + impact** — both prioridades equal weight (ex: Vox Capital, MOV)
- **Impact first** — impact prioridade, retorno sub-mercado aceito (ex: Yunus Social Business 0% retorno reinvestido)

**Asset classes impact 2026**:
1. **Venture capital impact** — early stage equity (Vox Capital, MOV Investimentos, Positive Ventures, Bemtevi)
2. **Private equity impact** — growth stage (Patria Impact, Adobe Capital)
3. **Debt impact** — credito direcionado (Sitawi Negocios Sociais, Banco Solidario Brasil)
4. **Public equities ESG/impact** — listed (BlackRock Impact, Itau Asset ESG)
5. **Outcome bonds** — pay-for-success (educacao, saude, prisao recidivismo)
6. **Microfinance** — credito base piramide (Banco Sol, Compartamos, no Brasil emerge)
7. **Green bonds / Social bonds** — debt instruments thematic (Brasil R$ 50bi+ emitidos 2024-2026)

**Impact bonds / outcome-based finance**:
- Investidor financia intervencao
- Government/foundation paga investidor SE outcome verificado independent evaluator
- Risk transfer governo → investidor privado
- Brasil pioneiro: Sitawi + Instituto Maria Cecilia Souto Vidigal + Yunus Social Business pilots

## Fundacao 7 — Anti-Patterns Impact-Washing (Greenwashing + Purpose-Washing + Impact Theater)

Impact-washing = epidemia 2025-2026. Investidores + jornalistas + reguladores cada vez mais skeptical. Founders genuinos sofrem com noise concorrente fake.

**Tipologia impact-washing**:

**1. Greenwashing** (ambiental):
- Claims verde sem verificacao terceiro
- Net-zero pledges 2050 sem milestones intermediarios
- Carbon offsets de baixa qualidade (REDD+ scam)
- Buzzwords "eco-friendly" sem definicao
- **Caso Brasil**: bancoes anunciando "compromisso clima" enquanto financiam desmatamento Amazonia

**2. Purpose-washing**:
- Purpose statement vacuous fabricado mkt
- "We're committed to making the world better"
- Backing zero proof points
- CEO contradicts em entrevistas
- Performative vs systemic

**3. Impact theater**:
- Eventos PR caros + impacto operacional zero
- "Day of giving" annual + 364 dias cinismo interno
- Fotos kids africanos volunteers — savior narrative
- Hashtag activism + zero policy/operational change

**4. Virtue signaling**:
- Brand ostensibly toma posicao causa
- Sem skin in the game (pricing, supply chain, hires, donations)
- Quando custo politico real chega, recua
- **Caso negativo cumple TOMS critique** — "buy one give one" criticado academicamente: shoes donated competiam local cobblers, deslocando empregos. TOMS 2019 reformou modelo (1/3 profits "people-first", measurable outcomes via partners verified)

**5. Tokenism**:
- Diversity hire visible (1 mulher conselho 100% homens) sem governance change
- Indigenous partnership tokenistic (1 sourcing relationship vs systemic)
- "Race-conscious" content mes black history + zero hiring/promotion

**6. ODS confetti**:
- Empresa mapeia para 17 ODS simultaneous (impossivel material)
- ODS as marketing decoration vs governance scaffold
- B Corp sem ODS material primary mapeada

**7. Founder story fabricated**:
- Origin story polished demais, perfect Hollywood arc
- Trigger event suspiciously timed marketing
- Vulnerability strategic vs authentic
- "I sold my Ferrari to start this" cringe trope

**8. Impact metrics vanity**:
- "Impacted 1 million lives" sem ToC + counterfactual
- "Saved X tons CO2" sem methodology
- "Created jobs" sem retention + wage quality
- IRIS+ ignored ou cherry-picked metrics

**9. B Corp shopping**:
- Empresa busca B Corp para PR sem internalizar governance change
- BIA scoring optimization gaming questions
- V2.1 standards 2026 mitigam mas nao eliminam

**10. Impact-driven sem impact-first willingness**:
- Empresa diz "impact-driven" mas qualquer trade-off retorno vs impact, retorno vence systematic
- Real impact-driven aceita 2-5% retorno menos por causa
- Investor base nao alinhada (returns first investor pressure)

**Detection tests jornalista/investidor 2026**:
- **Counterfactual test** — sem voce o impact ocorreria? (additionality)
- **Material question** — impact e core business model ou side effect?
- **Skin in the game** — founder/empresa carga real (financial, operational)?
- **Verification test** — third-party independent (B Lab, IRIS+, auditor)?
- **Stakeholder voice** — beneficiarios entrevistados + concordam impact narrative?
- **Scope 3 test** — supply chain auditado vs apenas operacao propria?
- **Compensation test** — exec comp tied impact metrics ou apenas financial?

## Anti-Patterns 18

1. **Purpose-washing vacuous** — "we're committed to making the world better" generico. Sem ToC + sem proof points + investor + jornalista detect imediato 2026.
2. **Greenwashing supply chain ignored** — Scope 1+2 verde + Scope 3 desmatamento Amazonia. CSRD vai expor.
3. **B Corp shopping para PR** — empresa busca seal SEM internalizar governance change. V2.1 mitiga mas nao elimina.
4. **ODS confetti** — mapeamento 17 ODS simultaneos. Material apenas 1-3 + 2-3 secundarios maximo.
5. **Founder story fabricated** — origin story Hollywood arc polished demais. Vulnerability strategic vs authentic detect.
6. **TOMS-style buy-one-give-one sem counterfactual** — pode deslocar economia local. Critique academica documentada.
7. **Impact metrics vanity** — "1 million lives impacted" sem ToC + counterfactual + IRIS+ alignment.
8. **IRIS+ cherry-picking** — selecionar metricas favoraveis ignorando material. Investidor sofisticado detect.
9. **Theory of Change ausente investor pitch** — pitch deck sem ToC slide explicit = founder pre-mature ou impact-washing.
10. **Returns first com impact veneer** — empresa diz "impact-driven" mas qualquer trade-off retorno vs impact, retorno sempre vence. Be honest sub-mercado tier.
11. **Endeavor confusion impact** — Endeavor e high-impact economico, NAO obrigatoriamente impact socioambiental. Marketing nao deve confundir.
12. **B Corp recertify negligenciado** — empresa certificada V1 ignora V2.1 transition deadline 2027-2028, perde seal silently.
13. **Aceleradora cherry-pick portfolio** — selecionar founders facil-impact sem additionality test. Ecossistema dilui.
14. **Impact bonds zero acquaintance** — founder ignora outcome-based finance options + governo Brasil contractor. Capital deixado mesa.
15. **Founder narrative LinkedIn corporate** — voice sanitized, LinkedIn algorithm 2026 punishes. Authentic > polished.
16. **Earned media generic Tier 1 attempt** — founder pre-PMF tenta Forbes/Valor cover. Wrong target. Pipe.Social listing + podcast G4 first.
17. **ASE/Endeavor application generic** — pitch material reciclado VC. Programs especificos exigem narrativa specific (ASE = systems change, Endeavor = scale).
18. **Conselho purpose-aligned ausente** — startup impact com 0 conselheiros independent ESG/impact background. Governance gap reputational.

## 18 Regras de Ouro

1. **Theory of Change explicit** — 1 pagina, problema systemic + beneficiarios + atividades + outcomes + counterfactual + assumptions.
2. **Intentionality + measurability + additionality** — 3 testes IMP canonicos.
3. **IRIS+ metrics 5-10 selecionadas** — desde dia 1, NAO retrofit posterior.
4. **ODS material 1-3 primarios + 2-3 secundarios** — NAO 17 simultaneous.
5. **B Corp considerada Serie A+** — pre-PMF premature, post-Series A bandwidth.
6. **B Corp V2.1 standards 2026+** — points-based legacy substituido por mandatory requirements 7 areas.
7. **Founder origin story authentic** — trigger event real + vulnerability + stakes + counterfactual.
8. **LinkedIn founder voice authentic** — algorithm 2026 favors. NAO ghostwriter sanitized pre-Series B.
9. **Investor pitch ToC slide explicit** — NAO esperar diligence question.
10. **Impact-first vs impact-driven honest** — sub-mercado vs mercado returns honesty para LP base.
11. **Pipe.Social + Quintessa Mapa** — listing + ecossistema visibility Brasil.
12. **Endeavor application strategic** — high-impact ECONOMICO foco, narrative differentiated impact-only programs.
13. **ASE Brasil systems change focus** — narrative diferente Endeavor (systems-level vs scale).
14. **Sebrae programs base** — Negocios de Impacto + InovAtiva + ALI consultoria, especialmente regiao Norte/Nordeste/Centro-Oeste sub-served.
15. **Investidor impact native — Vox/MOV/Positive/Bemtevi** — match founder stage + thesis vs generic VC.
