## Fundacao 1 — Crisis Communication Framework Historico (Tylenol J&J + Coombs SCCT + Benoit Image Repair)

**Tylenol/Johnson & Johnson 1982** = canonical case crisis communication moderna. 7 mortes Chicago area por capsulas Tylenol contaminadas com cianeto (terceira parte tampering, NAO falha J&J). J&J response virou playbook 40 anos:
- **Day 1**: CEO James Burke assume leadership pessoal, NAO delega
- **Day 2-3**: Recall nacional 31 milhoes frascos (US$ 100M custo direto, equivalente US$ 320M 2026), suspende producao, cessou propaganda
- **Day 4-7**: Press conferences daily com data + transparency, hotline 1-800 cliente
- **Week 2-3**: Cooperacao FBI total, oferta US$ 100k recompensa
- **Month 2**: Reintroducao com tamper-proof packaging (3-layer seal — pioneiro industria)
- **Resultado**: Market share recovery 80% em 8 semanas, 100% em 1 ano

**Tylenol lessons codificados**:
1. **Customer-first acima profit** — recall imediato apesar custo bilionario (NAO esperar regulatorio mandar)
2. **CEO visible leadership** — Burke pessoal, nao delegou advogado/PR
3. **Transparency total** — daily updates mesmo quando incompleto
4. **Cooperation regulators** — FBI/FDA/police informados antes minimizar
5. **Structural change** — tamper-proof packaging permanent fix
6. **Communication cadence** — daily press conf primeiras 2 semanas
7. **Apology + accountability + action** — formula 3-A canonical

**Coombs SCCT (Situational Crisis Communication Theory) 2007** — Tim Coombs Texas A&M:
- **Crisis types 3 clusters**:
  - **Victim cluster** (low responsibility) — natural disaster, workplace violence externa, malevolence terceiros (ex: Tylenol tampering)
  - **Accidental cluster** (medium) — challenge stakeholder, technical-error accident, technical-error product
  - **Preventable cluster** (high) — human-error accident, human-error product, organizational misdeed (ex: BP Deepwater 2010, VW Dieselgate 2015, Equifax 2017)
- **Response strategies escalating**:
  - **Deny** — denial, attack accuser, scapegoat (high risk, only se factualmente certo nao culpado)
  - **Diminish** — excuse (no intent), justification (minimal damage)
  - **Rebuild** — compensation, apology
  - **Bolstering** — reminder past good, ingratiation, victimage

**Benoit Image Repair Theory 1995** — William Benoit Missouri:
- **Denial** — simple denial OR shift blame
- **Evading responsibility** — provocation, defeasibility (lack info), accident, good intentions
- **Reducing offensiveness** — bolstering, minimization, differentiation, transcendence (higher purpose), attack accuser, compensation
- **Corrective action** — fix problem + prevent recurrence
- **Mortification** — full apology + accept responsibility

**Kategorizacao 2026 enriquecida**:
- **Cyber breach crisis** (Equifax 2017, Marriott 2018, Target 2013, Uber 2016 hidden breach)
- **Product safety crisis** (Tylenol 1982, Toyota 2010 sticky pedal, Boeing 737 MAX 2018-2019, Ypê 2024-2026 BR Pseudomonas)
- **Executive misconduct** (Uber Travis Kalanick 2017, McDonald's Steve Easterbrook 2019, BP Tony Hayward 2010)
- **Operational disaster** (BP Deepwater Horizon 2010, Norfolk Southern East Palestine 2023)
- **Social/cultural crisis** (Gillette toxic masculinity ad 2019, Pepsi Kendall Jenner 2017, Bud Light Dylan Mulvaney 2023)
- **AI/deepfake crisis** (emergente 2024-2026 — fake CEO audio/video viralizando)
- **Activist boycott crisis** (Chick-fil-A LGBTQ+, Goya Hispanic, Disney Don't Say Gay)

**Frameworks teste cluster**:
- **Responsibility test** — pode evidenciar terceiro causou? OR culpa interna?
- **Severity test** — vidas + saude + finanças stakeholders impactadas?
- **Intentionality test** — incident foi result de decisao deliberada?
- **History test** — repetido offense aumenta cluster severity (Coombs)

## Fundacao 2 — Crisis Playbook 2026 (Pre-Crisis + Dark Site + Holding Statements + Spokesperson)

**Crisis playbook** = documento operacional 30-60 paginas, NAO theoretical. Structure canonical 2026:

**Section 1: Crisis taxonomy + escalation matrix**
- **Tipos crise priorizadas** organizacao-specific (top 10-15 cenarios provaveis)
- **Severity levels**: L1 (issue), L2 (concern), L3 (crisis), L4 (catastrophe)
- **Escalation criteria** explicitas: numero customers afetados, mention threshold (>500 mentions/h), media tier (Tier 1 Valor/Estadao/Globo = auto-L3+)

**Section 2: Crisis response team (CRT)**
- **Crisis leader** (CEO ou COO designado pre-crisis) — DRI ultima decision
- **Spokesperson principal** + backup (NUNCA CEO se materia juridica/financial — CFO ou General Counsel)
- **Communications lead** — CCO ou Head Comm
- **Legal lead** — General Counsel ou external firm partner
- **Operations lead** — COO ou functional VP
- **HR/People lead** — CHRO (employee comms + safety)
- **Customer/CX lead** — VP Customer
- **IT/Security lead** — CISO (cyber crises)
- **DPO** (LGPD compliance — Brasil mandatory)
- **Agency partner** (external crisis comm firm on-retainer)
- **Roles + responsibilities matrix** (RACI explicit)

**Section 3: Activation procedures**
- **Trigger criteria + alert paths** — quem alerta quem, em quanto tempo
- **War room logistics** — physical (HQ conference room dedicated) + virtual (Slack channel + Zoom bridge persistente)
- **First 60-min checklist** (golden hour):
  1. Verify facts (quem afetado, quando, magnitude)
  2. Activate CRT (call tree pre-defined)
  3. Assign single spokesperson + decision-maker
  4. Issue holding statement (within 30-60 min)
  5. Activate dark site
  6. Limit approvals to 15-min cycles maximum

**Section 4: Holding statements pre-approved**
3 holding statements minimo pre-drafted + approved legal/PR/CEO:
- **Service outage holding statement** (template):
  > "We are aware of [issue] affecting [scope]. Our team is actively investigating. We will provide updates every [30/60] minutes via [channels]. Customer safety is our priority. [Contact info hotline]."
- **Data breach holding statement** (template):
  > "We have detected unauthorized access to [system]. We have engaged [forensics firm] and notified [authorities/ANPD]. We are working to determine scope. Affected customers will be notified directly within [timeframe]. [Hotline] [Dark site URL]."
- **Product safety holding statement** (template):
  > "We have received reports of [issue] with [product] [batch X]. As a precaution, we are [recall/suspend]. Customers should [stop using/return]. We are cooperating fully with [Anvisa/regulator]. [Hotline] [Dark site URL]."

**Section 5: Dark site (microsite crise)**
- **Dark site** = pre-built non-indexed landing page que substitui homepage durante crise
- **Pre-loaded assets**: holding statements drafts, executive bios + photos, fact sheets, FAQ templates, contact info hotline, regulatory cooperation statement, history company safety/quality, news release templates
- **Replaces marketing content** — homepage redirected to dark site durante L3+ crisis
- **Multi-channel**: web + landing page social + email signature update + Google Ad freeze
- **Cases**: Marriott 2018 (500M records breach) ativou dark site em <4h — referencia pratica. Equifax 2017 contraexemplo — site equifaxsecurity2017.com lento, layout caotico, virou meme negativo amplificou crise

**Section 6: Spokesperson selection + media training**
- **CEO speaks when**: human safety affected, multiple deaths, intentional misconduct disclosure, structural reform announcement (Tylenol model)
- **CFO/General Counsel speaks when**: financial materiality, legal proceedings, regulatory inquiry, securities matter
- **Operations VP speaks when**: operational/technical issue, customer service
- **CISO speaks when**: cyber breach (com General Counsel)
- **DPO speaks when**: LGPD/data privacy specific (Brasil)
- **NEVER**: junior PR rep speaks Tier 1 crisis (credibility gap)
- **Media training mandatory**:
  - Refresh anual (CEO + spokesperson team)
  - Pre-crisis simulation (mock interviews + tough questions)
  - Bridging techniques + STAR method
  - Body language + tone
  - On/off record discipline

**Section 7: Approval cycles + cadence**
- **15-min approval max** durante L3+ — NAO esperar full board approval messaging
- **Update cadence**:
  - Hour 1-4: every 15-30 min
  - Hour 4-24: every hour
  - Day 2-7: every 4-8 hours
  - Week 2-4: daily
  - Month 2+: weekly to recovery

**Section 8: Post-crisis review + after-action report (AAR)**
- **24h hot wash** — immediate lessons CRT
- **30-day AAR** — formal analysis: timeline, decisions, gaps, fixes
- **Quarterly playbook update** — incorporate lessons learned
- **Annual simulation 2x** — table-top + full activation drill

## Fundacao 3 — Social Media Crisis 2026 (Golden 15 Minutes + Viral Cycle + AI Deepfakes)

**Social media crisis 2026** = different beast vs press crisis classica. Velocidade + AI amplification + permanence = crisis modernity.

**Golden 15 Minutes** (2026 reduzido de "Golden Hour" 2015-2020):
- **0-15 min**: Acknowledge presence (mesmo se details unknown). Algoritmos amplificam negatividade default — silencio = endorsement implicito da narrativa critica.
- **15-60 min**: Holding statement public + dark site activated
- **1-2h**: Substantive update (facts known + actions taken)
- **2-4h**: Public narrative solidifies — apos 4h impossivel reescrever
- **24-72h**: Recovery messaging + structural action
- **Brands falhando responder 60 min**: 12% drop revenue trimestral medio (industry benchmark)

**Viral cycle 2026**:
- **Single tweet/post** vira trending em <2h se reaches influencer >100k followers
- **Reddit thread** atinge front page r/all overnight (10M+ views possivel)
- **TikTok stitch/duet** amplifica original 10-100x em 24h
- **News pickup** — Tier 2 outlets (BuzzFeed, Vice, NeoFeed) pegam viral em 4-12h, Tier 1 (NYT, Globo, Folha) em 24-48h
- **AI engines citacao** — ChatGPT/Perplexity/Gemini citam viral threads como fact por meses pos-evento (Reddit threads = top training data Google AI Overview)
- **Permanence Wikipedia** — Wikipedia article incident criado em 24-72h, vira reference top Google AI summaries

**AI deepfakes crisis emergente 2024-2026**:
- Fake CEO audio (5-30s clip vai viral) — dificil distinguir audio real
- Fake earnings statement video — manipulado stock impact
- Fake employee abuse video synthetic
- **Response framework**:
  - Identify deepfake (forensic tools — Reality Defender, Truepic, Sensity AI)
  - Issue immediate denial + provide authentic comparison
  - Engage platforms (X, Meta, TikTok, YouTube) takedown
  - Publish forensic proof publica
  - Legal action if criminal intent identified
- **Pre-empt strategy**: CEO pre-recorded "verification kit" (canonical voice + video samples + biometric registry) ready forensic comparison

**Social listening tools 2026**:
- **Brandwatch** — sentiment + crisis alerts realtime
- **Sprinklr** — unified social + alerts
- **Talkwalker** — conversation analytics + virality prediction
- **Meltwater** — media + social + influencer tracking
- **Cision Social** — integrated PR + social
- **Zignal Labs** — narrative intelligence + AI
- **Hootsuite Insights** — accessible mid-market
- **Mention** — entry-level monitoring
- **Brasil-specific**: Stilingue, Bites, Cortex Intelligence, MediaMonitors

**Alert thresholds 2026**:
- **L1 issue**: <100 mentions/h, neutral/mixed sentiment
- **L2 concern**: 100-500 mentions/h, sentiment turning negative >40%
- **L3 crisis**: >500 mentions/h, negative >60%, influencer >100k engaged
- **L4 catastrophe**: >5000 mentions/h, Tier 1 media pickup, regulatory mention, viral video >1M views

**Response disciplines social media crise**:
- **NAO deletar comments criticos** (Streisand effect amplifica)
- **NAO debater haters publicly** (engagement aumenta visibility)
- **Respond pinned post + hold thread** (keep conversation centralized)
- **Pause organic + paid social** todos posts non-crisis durante L3+
- **Update bio + pinned tweet/post** com link dark site
- **DM customers afetados individuamente** (escala onde possivel)
- **Cross-platform consistency** mensagem (X + LinkedIn + Instagram + Facebook + TikTok)

## Fundacao 4 — LGPD/ANPD Breach Notification Brasil 2026

**LGPD (Lei Geral de Protecao de Dados) Brasil** Lei 13.709/2018 efetivo Sept 2020. ANPD (Autoridade Nacional Protecao Dados) reguladora. Resolucao CD/ANPD 15/2024 consolidou regime breach notification.

**Quando notificar**:
- **Data breach LGPD**: "qualquer evento adverso confirmado, relacionado a violacao de seguranca, que acarretou destruicao, perda, alteracao, comunicacao ou difusao nao autorizada de dados pessoais"
- **Trigger notification**: incident "que possa acarretar risco ou dano relevante aos titulares" — risk-based, NAO toda breach
- **Risk relevante** = consideracoes ANPD: tipo dado (dados sensiveis aumentam risk), volume titulares afetados, criancas/adolescentes/idosos envolvidos, possibilidade danos materiais/morais, facilidade identificacao/reidentificacao

**Quando notificar (timing)**:
- **3 dias uteis** apos controlador tomar conhecimento (NAO 72h calendar como GDPR)
- **DIFERENCA CRUCIAL vs GDPR**: GDPR 72h fixed, LGPD 3 working days — pode estender 3-5 calendar days (com fim semana)
- **Notification platform**: SUPER (Sistema de Comunicacao de Incidentes) — plataforma online ANPD desde 2024
- **Notificar ANPD + titulares afetados** ambos quando risco relevante

**Conteudo notification ANPD mandatory**:
1. **Descricao natureza** dados pessoais afetados
2. **Informacao titulares** envolvidos — quantidade, detalhe se criancas/adolescentes/idosos
3. **Data incident** (se identificable) + data conhecimento
4. **Tecnicas seguranca** anteriores ao incident
5. **Riscos possiveis** aos titulares
6. **Razoes atraso** notification (se >3 dias uteis)
7. **Medidas corretivas** adotadas / a serem adotadas
8. **Informacao DPO** ou representante legal controlador
9. **Quantidade total titulares** afetados

**Data retention obligation**:
- Manter registro TODAS data breaches (mesmo nao notificadas) por minimo 5 anos
- Documento detalhado natureza, escopo, medidas mitigacao

**Multas LGPD 2026**:
- Ate **2% faturamento empresa Brasil** ano anterior, limitado **R$ 50 milhoes por infracao**
- Multa diaria possivel
- Publicizacao infracao
- Bloqueio dados pessoais
- Eliminacao dados pessoais

**ANPD enforcement record 2024-2026**:
- ANPD ainda aplicando multas conservadoramente vs CNIL (Franca) ou ICO (UK)
- Casos concluidos: Telefonica/Vivo R$ 2.6M (2023), CCAA R$ 80k (2023), JBS R$ 200k (2024), Cebraspe (2024)
- Trend 2026: ANPD aumentou volume notificacoes recebidas (1500+/ano), enforcement escalando

**Communication plan integrated LGPD breach**:
- **T+0 (deteccao)**: CISO + DPO + Legal alerted, IT forensics ativado, evidence preservation
- **T+1h**: Internal CRT activated, scope assessment, legal evaluation "risco relevante"
- **T+24h**: Internal communication employees (LGPD says employee data protection), customer communication preparation
- **T+48-72h**: Decision notification ANPD (legal/DPO call) + draft customer notifications
- **T+72h (3 working days)**: ANPD notification submitted SUPER + start customer notifications
- **T+1week**: Public statement (if media exposure), dark site activated
- **T+2week**: Detailed FAQ + remediation actions communicated
- **T+30 days**: Full incident report ANPD if requested
- **T+90 days**: AAR + structural changes + governance review

**Brasil cases LGPD breach 2024-2026**:
- **Cyrela Brazil Realty (2023)**: incident notificado ANPD, response transparent
- **Inter (Banco Inter, 2023)**: vazamento dados clientes, ANPD investigando
- **Marisa (2024)**: ransomware impactou dados clientes
- **Pix-related fraud incidents** ongoing 2024-2026

**Cross-border LGPD scenarios**:
- Multi-juridicao breach (LGPD + GDPR + CCPA + PIPL): coordenar notifications timeline mais restrictive
- US PCI-DSS + LGPD overlap healthcare/finance
- Cross-border data transfer LGPD post-Sept 2024 ANPD regulamentacao

**DPO obrigatorio** (LGPD Art. 41):
- Encarregado dados — pessoa fisica/juridica designado
- Coordena notifications ANPD + titulares
- Crisis comm legal anchor

## Fundacao 5 — Reputation Recovery (RepTrak + Edelman + Time-to-Trust)

**Reputation recovery** = phase pos-crisis. Tipicamente 2-4 anos full recovery (RepTrak baseline), mas excepcional 6-18 meses (Tylenol, Domino's pizza 2009).

**RepTrak Reputation Index** — Charles Fombrun + RepTrak Company:
- 7 dimensoes RepTrak Pulse:
  1. **Products/Services** — quality, value
  2. **Innovation** — leadership tech/process
  3. **Workplace** — employee treatment
  4. **Governance** — ethics, transparency
  5. **Citizenship** — community, environment, ESG
  6. **Leadership** — vision, executives
  7. **Performance** — financial results
- **Reputation score 0-100**, benchmark setor
- **Crisis impact**: typical drop 8-20 points L3 crisis, 30-50 points L4 catastrophic
- **Recovery curve**: linear NAO, dependent structural changes

**Edelman Trust Barometer 2026** key insights:
- **70% pessoas em "insular mindset"** — economic anxiety + geopolitical tension + tech disruption (Edelman 2026 global)
- **75% pessoas NAO confiam brands pos-crise** (industry baseline)
- **87% perdoam SE company demonstra transparency + accountability** (recovery framework)
- **"Trust brokering"** — finding common ground, nonjudgmental communication (Edelman 2026 prescription)
- **"Polynational" business models** — long-term local relationships investment
- Trust hierarchy 2026: family > scientists > local NGOs > business > government > media (Edelman annual)

**Trust recovery framework** (Edelman + RepTrak synthesized):
1. **Acknowledgment** (T+0-7 days)
   - Public apology unequivocal (NO hedging)
   - Accept responsibility (mortification mode Benoit if culpa)
   - Express empathy victims/affected
   - Personal CEO leadership visible
2. **Accountability** (T+1-4 weeks)
   - Investigation independent (third-party preferred)
   - Immediate remediation actions visible
   - Compensation/restitution stakeholders affected
   - Transparency findings (warts and all)
3. **Action** (T+1-12 months)
   - Structural changes (governance, process, leadership turnover if needed)
   - Process improvements verified externamente
   - Industry leadership safety/standards (Tylenol tamper-proof model)
   - Continued cadence updates (NAO desaparecer)
4. **Affirmation** (T+12-36 months)
   - Brand relaunch when ready (NAO premature)
   - Owned content storytelling reform journey
   - Earned media positive reframing
   - Employee advocacy authentic
   - Customer testimonials repaired relationships

- **Itau Unibanco** — sector leader, crisis-resilient
- **Magazine Luiza** — Lu mascot + Luiza Trajano leadership = trust premium

**Time-to-recovery accelerators**:
- CEO personal leadership (Tylenol model)
- Structural change visible
- Independent verification (third-party audits)
- Compensation generous (vs minimum legal)
- Industry standard-setting (turn problem into leadership)
- Authentic acknowledgment (NOT lawyered apologies)

**Time-to-recovery decelerators**:
- Defensive legal posture
- Executive turnover insufficient (when accountability requires)
- Repeated incidents (Coombs history multiplier)
- Lawsuit settlements without admission
- Symbolic actions vs structural

## Fundacao 7 — CEO Apology Framework + Holding Statements + Spokesperson Selection

**CEO apology framework** = high-stakes communication. Errar = amplify crisis. Acertar = accelerate recovery.

**When CEO must apologize**:
- Loss of human life (always)
- Multiple injuries / safety failures
- Intentional misconduct disclosure
- Trust violation foundational (deception)
- Stakeholder consensus (employees + customers + board signal)
- Cluster Coombs preventable severity high
- Brand-level damage (NAO produto-level)

**When CEO should NOT apologize personally**:
- Pure operational/technical issue (escalate to COO/VP)
- Minor product issue (escalate to brand manager)
- Litigation in progress (legal counsel guidance)
- Cluster Coombs victim (third-party caused) — pode appear weak/conceding fault
- Pre-trial (EUA Securities risk) — CFO/General Counsel preferred

**Anatomy effective CEO apology**:
1. **Open with empathy** ("My heart goes out to families affected" / "We are deeply sorry")
2. **Acknowledge specific harm** (NOT vague "any inconvenience" hedging)
3. **Take responsibility** ("We failed [specifically how]" — NAO blame third party even if true)
4. **Provide facts** (what happened, what is known, what is unknown)
5. **State immediate actions** (recall, suspension, investigation)
6. **Promise structural change** (with specific commitment)
7. **Provide contact info** (hotline, dark site, dedicated email)
8. **Sign off personally** (NOT corporate "we")
9. **Commit follow-up cadence** (next update timing)

**Format CEO apology 2026**:
- **Video** > written statement (humanity, eye contact, tone heard)
- **Personal recording** > polished produced (authenticity premium 2026)
- **Direct CEO** > spokesperson reading (delegation perceived as evasion)
- **30-90 segundos** optimal (longer dilutes, shorter inadequate)
- **Subtitled** mandatory accessibility + AI transcription
- **Multi-platform** (LinkedIn + YouTube + dark site + X)
- **NO music, NO graphics, NO production** during initial — austere honest

**CEO apology DO**:
- "We are sorry" (unhedged)
- "We failed" (specific accountability)
- "We will" (forward action)
- Eye contact camera
- Slow measured pace
- Pause for emphasis
- Personal pronouns ("I", "we" — NOT "the company")
- Specific stakeholders named (employees, customers, families, communities)

**CEO apology DON'T**:
- "Mistakes were made" (passive voice classic non-apology)
- "If anyone was offended" (hedging implies critic fault)
- "We're sorry you feel that way" (deflects responsibility)
- "Out of an abundance of caution" (legal hedge, perceived as weasel)
- "Industry-wide issue" (deflects to others)
- Reading from teleprompter (perceived inauthentic)
- Background music (perceived produced/insincere)
- Defensive body language (crossed arms, looking away)
- Lawyer-drafted language verbatim ("alleged", "purported")

**Holding statements types** (pre-approved CRT):

**Type 1 — Acknowledge + investigate**:
> "We are aware of [issue] reported [today/this morning] affecting [scope]. We are taking this seriously and have activated our crisis response team. We are working to understand the full scope and will share updates as we learn more. Our next update will be at [TIME]."

**Type 2 — Acknowledge + immediate action**:
> "We have identified [issue] affecting [scope]. As a precaution, we have [recall/suspended service/notified authorities]. Affected customers should [action]. We sincerely apologize for the disruption. Updates: [dark site URL]. Hotline: [number]."

**Type 3 — Apologize + commit**:
> "We deeply regret [incident] and apologize unreservedly to those affected. We are working with [authorities/forensics/independent experts] to understand what happened and prevent recurrence. We commit to [specific actions]. CEO statement at [time]: [link]."

**Type 4 — Data breach LGPD-specific Brasil**:
> "Identificamos acesso nao autorizado a [sistema] em [data]. Imediatamente engajamos [forense + advogados] e notificamos a Autoridade Nacional de Protecao de Dados (ANPD) conforme LGPD. Estamos trabalhando para determinar escopo. Clientes afetados serao notificados diretamente em ate [prazo]. DPO: [nome] [email]. Atualizacoes: [dark site URL]. Linha direta: [telefone]."

**Spokesperson decision matrix**:

| Situacao | Spokesperson primario | Backup |
|----------|----------------------|--------|
| Mortes/lesoes humanas | CEO | COO + medical/safety expert |
| Data breach LGPD | CISO + DPO | General Counsel |
| Material financial event | CFO | CEO + General Counsel |
| Litigacao ativa | General Counsel | CFO |
| Operacional/tecnico | COO ou VP funcional | CTO |
| Product safety/recall | VP Quality | COO |
| Executive misconduct | Board Chair | CEO sucessor |
| Corporate culture/values | CEO | CHRO |
| Layoff/restructuring | CEO + CHRO | COO |
| ESG/sustainability | CEO + Chief Sustainability | CSR head |
| AI deepfake | CEO authentic + CISO | General Counsel |

**Spokesperson briefing checklist**:
- Latest verified facts (timestamp every fact)
- Approved messages 3-5 key (memorize)
- Bridging phrases ("What I can tell you is..." / "What's most important...")
- Off-limits topics (litigation specifics, individuals identifiable, financials un-released)
- Emotional preparation (this WILL be hostile)
- Wardrobe/setting (somber, not corporate slick)
- Recording for review post-event

## Anti-Patterns 18

1. **No playbook improvise** — sem pre-built playbook, organizacao improvisa pos-incidente. Time-to-response slow + inconsistente + decisions panic-driven.
2. **CEO ghost during crisis** — CEO ausente primeiros 48h, delega comm a junior PR. Trust vacuum competidor preenche, recovery extended 2-3x.
3. **Lawyered statements** — statements lawyer-drafted "alleged/purported" jargon. Audience perceives evasion, amplifies crisis vs minimiza.
4. **"Mistakes were made" passive voice** — non-apology classic. 2026 audiences detect immediately, viral mockery.
5. **Streisand effect deletes** — deletar comments criticos amplifica visibility 10-100x (Streisand 2003 founding case).
6. **Dark site tarde/quebrado** — Equifax 2017 referencia negativa. Dark site lento, layout caotico = meme + crisis amplificada.
7. **Holding statement vague generic** — "We take this seriously" sem specifics = perceived as PR speak vacuous.
8. **Spokesperson rotation inconsistent** — multiple voices contradicting (CFO X, CEO Y, PR Z). Trust collapse acelerado.
9. **Slow to acknowledge social media** — apos golden 15 min, narrativa publica solidifica adverse, irreversivel apos 4h.
10. **Defensive legal posture sole** — silent defensive crouch. Audience reads "guilty + hiding". Recovery decelerated.
11. **No CEO apology when warranted** — refusing apology cluster Coombs preventable. Brand permanente damage.
12. **LGPD ANPD notification atrasada** — past 3 working days = enforcement action + reputational compounding.
13. **Internal employees ignored** — comm only external. Employees Glassdoor/Indeed leak destroys employer brand recovery.
14. **AI deepfake unprepared** — sem verification kit pre-recorded, sem forensic protocols. Deepfake takes 24-72h vacuum to debunk.
15. **No structural change communicated** — words without action. Recovery never crosses Affirmation phase.
16. **Apology video produced over-polished** — music + graphics + makeup studio = perceived insincere. Authentic austere wins.
17. **Repeat offense same root cause** — Coombs history multiplier effect. Trust collapse exponential vs linear.
18. **No after-action review** — same crisis pattern repeats. Organizational learning failure compounds reputation cost.

## 18 Regras de Ouro

1. **Playbook pre-built mandatory** — 30-60 paginas (enterprise) ou 5-10 (startup). Atualizado quarterly.
2. **Golden 15 minutes** — acknowledge presence em 15 min mesmo sem detalhes. Holding statement em 60 min.
3. **CEO visible Tier 1 crises** — Tylenol model: human safety = CEO pessoal, NO delegation.
4. **Transparency > polish** — facts incomplete OK, evasion never. Authenticity premium 2026.
5. **Apologize when warranted unhedged** — "We are sorry. We failed. We will." Sem "if/any/feel".
6. **Single spokesperson + backup** — consistency vs rotation chaos.
7. **Dark site ready + tested** — quarterly deployment drill. NAO Equifax-failure.
8. **Holding statements library** — 5-10 templates pre-approved CRT.
9. **LGPD ANPD 3 working days** — Brasil Resolucao CD/ANPD 15/2024 mandatory.
10. **Cadence updates** — every 15-30 min hour 1-4, hourly day 1, daily week 1, weekly month 1.
11. **Structural change visible** — Tylenol tamper-proof packaging model. Words without action recovery falha.
12. **Independent verification** — third-party audits + forensics. Internal investigation insuficiente Tier 1.
13. **Employee internal first** — employees primeiro before public statement onde feasible. Glassdoor protection.
14. **AI deepfake verification kit** — CEO pre-recorded biometric + voice samples ready forensic.
15. **Annual simulations 2x** — table-top + full activation. Muscle memory vs panic.
16. **AAR post-incident 30-day formal** — lessons + playbook updates + structural fixes.
17. **Brasil cultural codes** — LGPD/ANPD compliance + Anvisa/CVM regulators + ABRACOM standards + cultural directness BR.
18. **Disclaimer mandatory educational** — content publico nao juridico LGPD/CVM/regulators.
